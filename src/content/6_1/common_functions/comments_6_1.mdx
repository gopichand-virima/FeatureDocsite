## Comments

Adding a Comment

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click the **Comments** tab.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

In the Comments window, click **New Comment**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/add-comment.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 334px;height: 92px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/add-comment-new.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 331px;height: 94px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter the applicable text.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" style="font-weight: normal;" madcap:autonum="4. ">

To add any documents, click **Attach**, and search for the attachment.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Click **Add** to save the comment and any attachments.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

To leave the comment window without adding any comments or attachments, click **Cancel**.

</div>

Editing a Comment

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

While viewing the **Comment** tab, find the comment to edit.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click the **pencil** icon. A comment field displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/client-comment-edit.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 307px;height: 132px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/client-comment-edit-new.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 316px;height: 117px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Edit the comment, as necessary.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

If applicable, attach any files.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Click **Save**. The new text replaces the existing text.

</div>

Deleting a Comment

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

While viewing the Comments tab, find the comment to delete.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click the **trashcan** icon

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the confirmation window, click **Yes**.

</div>

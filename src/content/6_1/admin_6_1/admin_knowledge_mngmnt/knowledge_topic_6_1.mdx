# Knowledge Topic

Use this function to configure a topic for knowledge sharing purposes. It can also be used to resolve frequently occurring errors.

In the main window, click **Admin /> Knowledge Management /> Knowledge Topic**.  The Knowledge Topic window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/knowledge-topic-main-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

In the main window, click the **gear** icon and select **Knowledge Management /> Knowledge Topic**.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/icon-knowledge-topic.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

The Knowledge Topic window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/knowledge-topic-main-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

New Knowledge Topic

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Knowledge Topic**. The New Knowledge Topic window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/knowledge-topic-new-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **New**. The New Knowledge Topic window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/knowledge-topic-new-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter a *Name* and *Description* for the topic.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Click **Add**.

</div>

### SubTopic

Use this function to add sub topic for a Knowledge Topic. The configured sub topic will be displayed as an option while configuring a Knowledge Topic.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

While viewing the Knowledge Topic Details window, click the **SubTopic** tab.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/knowledge-topic-subtopic-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/knowledge-topic-subtopic-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **New Knowledge Sub Topic**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Click **New**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/knowledge-topic-subtopic-add-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Enter a *Name* and *Description*.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Click **Add**.

</div>

Once a sub topic is added, either of the following actions can be performed:

<div class="Bullet1" madcap:autonum="">

Click **Export** to generate a spreadsheet and create an email notification that is set to the logged in user with a link to download a spreadsheet with the data.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1" madcap:autonum="">

Click **Delete** to remove the subtopic.

</div>

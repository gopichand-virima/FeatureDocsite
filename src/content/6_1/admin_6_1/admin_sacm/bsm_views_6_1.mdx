# BSM View

Use this function to customize the display properties of Business Service Management (BSM).

In the main window, click **Admin /> SACM /> BSM View**. The BSM View window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20SACM/bsm-view-old.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 680px;height: 114px;"></span>

In the main window, click the **gear** icon and select **SACM /> BSM View**.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20SACM/icon-custom-bsm-views.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

The BSM Views window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20SACM/bsm-view-new.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 613px;height: 296px;"></span>

New BSM View

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose <span style="background-color: #ffffff; font-weight: bold;">New BSM View</span>.

</div>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **New BSM View**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter a *Name* for the BSM view.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

From the *Include Relationships* and *Include Blueprints* drop-down lists, select the type of required relationship and blueprint.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

From the *Exclude Relationships* and *Exclude Blueprints* drop-down lists, select the type of relationship and blueprint to be omitted.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Check *Include External CI Communication* option to include external communications to the corresponding CI.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

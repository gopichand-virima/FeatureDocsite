# Risk Register - Online Help Overview

Welcome to the Risk Register module documentation for Virima version 5.13.

## Overview

The Risk Register module enables organizations to identify, assess, and manage IT risks systematically, ensuring proactive risk mitigation and compliance with governance requirements.

## Key Features

- **Risk Identification**: Document and categorize potential risks
- **Risk Assessment**: Evaluate probability and impact
- **Risk Mitigation Planning**: Define and track mitigation actions
- **Risk Heat Maps**: Visual risk prioritization
- **Compliance Tracking**: Monitor regulatory compliance
- **Risk Reporting**: Generate comprehensive risk reports

## Getting Started

To begin managing risks:
1. Navigate to Risk Register from the main menu
2. Create new risk entries
3. Assess risk probability and impact
4. Define mitigation strategies
5. Assign risk owners and track actions

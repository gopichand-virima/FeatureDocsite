## Creating a Filter

<div class="numlist1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Under the column heading, click in the *Filter for /[name of filter/]* field.

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Type the text to filter by and press **Enter**. The filter is added to the criteria area. The window updates, and all matching records (if any) are displayed.

</div>

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Common%20Topics/saved-filter-search.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>  

</div>

Click the **Save Filter** text.

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

</div>

## Applying a Filter

Saved filters are used to sort results with previously entered search criteria. This eliminates the need to recreate a new filter each time a user wants to search the list of records.

<div class="numlist1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the results window, move to the upper right corner and click the **Saved Filter** icon. The Filters window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Common%20Topics/saved-filter-list.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Common%20Topics/saved-filters-new-list-delete.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 363px;height: 134px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Select the filter you want to apply from the list.

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

To save a filter as the default, click **Set as Default**. Only one filter can be the default.

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

To delete the filter, click the **trashcan** icon.

</div>

## Add a New Peripheral Device

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **Add**. The Peripheral Device dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/peripheral-device-add.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 427px;height: 120px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/peripheral-device-add-new.png" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 421px;height: 137px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **New Peripheral Device.**

</div>

<div class="NumList2" style="font-style: normal;" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter the applicable field information.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Save**.

</div>

## Modify Existing Peripheral Device

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **Existing Peripheral Device**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/peripheral-device-existing.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 476px;height: 164px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/peripheral-device-existing-new.png" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 473px;height: 322px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter the search criteria to locate the existing device.  *See also* <a href="../../../Common%20Topics/Advanced-Search.htm" style="font-style: italic;">Advanced Search</a>*.*

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Select the applicable device from the results list.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

When all selections are made, click **Save**.

</div>

## View and Edit the Peripheral Device Details

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

While viewing the list of existing devices, select the device to edit. The windows refreshes and the details for the device displays. These details are view only.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" style="font-style: normal;" madcap:autonum="2. ">

To view the supporting CIs:

</div>

<div class="NumLista" madcap:autonum="a. ">

Click the CI item.

</div>

<div class="Ind75">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/supporting-cis.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind75">

The Configuration Items window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumListb" madcap:autonum="b. ">

Click **Components**. *Refer to* <a href="CI-Comp-List.htm" style="font-style: italic;">CI Components</a>*.*

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

To add a contact, navigate to the Contacts section and click **Add**. The Contacts dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/network-adapaters-contacts.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 428px;height: 215px;" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/network-adapaters-contacts-new.png" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 432px;height: 333px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumLista" madcap:autonum="a. ">

In the *Choose Relation* field, click the drop-down list and select how this contact should be related to this asset.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="b. ">

In the search field, enter the search criteria.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="c. ">

Select a contact from the results list, moving it to the selected list.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="d. ">

When all selections are made, click **Save**.

</div>

## Delete Peripheral Device

While viewing the list of existing devices, select the device to delete, and click the **Delete** button.

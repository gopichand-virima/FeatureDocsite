# Details

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/CMDB%20-%20Left-Nav/Configuration%20Management%20Database_37.png" original-image-title=""></span>

- The <span class="Strong" style="font-weight: bold;">Details tab</span> provides the <span class="Strong" style="font-weight: bold;">primary information</span> for a Configuration Item (CI) and is the first section you see upon opening a CI record in the CMDB.

- It serves as the <span class="Strong" style="font-weight: bold;">core identity and profile view</span> of the asset, containing all critical fields related to the asset's configuration, lifecycle, and management metadata.

- The <span class="Strong" style="font-weight: bold;">Details view</span> consists of the following categories of data:

  - Asset primary information

  - Hardware and network

  - Operating system details

  - Supporting CIs

  - Contacts

  - Contracts

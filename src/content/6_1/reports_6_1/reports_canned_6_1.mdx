# Canned Reports

Use this function to generate canned reports for which formatting has been applied, making them presentable for display and distribution.

In the navigation pane, select **Reports /> Canned Reports**. The Reports window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Reports/canned-report-main-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Reports/canned-report-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

New Canned Report

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose [New Canned Report](Reports-New-Canned.htm).

To create a new report, click [New Report](Reports-New-Canned.htm).

# Prerequisites

To run discovery scans that populate the **Recent Scans** page:

- **Discovery Client (Server) Configuration** – At least one discovery client must be installed and active. (Admin > Discovery > Clients)
- **Credentials** – Valid credentials (SNMP, SSH, WMI, VMware, etc.) must be configured. (Admin > Discovery > Credentials)
- **Scan Configuration** – A scan must be initiated via **Run a Scan** or **Scheduled Scans**.

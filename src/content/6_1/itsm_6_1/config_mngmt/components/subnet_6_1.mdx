# Subnets

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

While viewing an asset record, click **Components**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **Subnets**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/subnets-new.png" original-image-title="" style="width: 475px;height: 172px;"></span>

</div>

## Add New Subnet

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **Add**. The Subnet dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/subnets-add-new.png" original-image-title="" style="width: 478px;height: 157px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **New Subnet** (if not already selected).

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter the applicable information in each field.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

When all entries are made, click **Save**.

</div>

## Modify Existing Subnet

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **Existing Subnet**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/subnets-existing-new.png" original-image-title="" style="width: 478px;height: 329px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter the search criteria to locate the existing device. *See also <a href="../../../Common%20Topics/Advanced-Search.htm" target="_popup">Advanced Search</a>.*

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Select the applicable device from the results list.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Save**.

</div>

## Delete Subnet

While viewing the list of existing subnets, select the device to delete, and click the **Delete** button.

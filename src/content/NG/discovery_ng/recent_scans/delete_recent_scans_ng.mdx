# Deleting Recent Scans

**Recent Scans can be deleted.**

- Select a scan record.
- From the **Select Actions** dropdown, choose **Delete**.
- Confirm deletion when prompted.

**Effect of deletion:**

- The scan entry will be removed from the **Recent Scans** list.
- Any discovered items associated with that scan will remain in the **Discovered Items** page unless separately deleted.

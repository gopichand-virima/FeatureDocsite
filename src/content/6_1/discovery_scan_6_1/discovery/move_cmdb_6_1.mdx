# Moving Assets to CMBD

<span class="span_5">Use this function to view the assets discovered after the scan and move them to the CMDB.</span>

<table class="TableStyle-Important" style="mc-table-style: url(&#39;../Resources/TableStyles/Important.css&#39;);margin-left: 0;margin-right: auto;width: 100%;" data-cellspacing="0">
<tbody>
<tr class="TableStyle-Important-Body-Body1">
<td class="TableStyle-Important-BodyB-Column1-Body1" style="text-align: center;"> </td>
<td class="TableStyle-Important-BodyA-Column2-Body1"><p>An asset record can also be moved from the <span>Details </span>window. However, wait until the scan is complete before initiating a move operation. Also, once a record is moved, it cannot be retrieved.</p>
<p>If the move appears to be unsuccessful, contact your System Administrator.</p></td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the navigation pane, select <span class="Strong_4" style="font-weight: bold;">Discovery Scan</span><span class="span_5" style="font-weight: bold;"> /> </span><span class="Strong_4" style="font-weight: bold;">Discovered Items</span>. The Discovered Items window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Discovery/discovered-items-main-old.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" style="width: 622px;height: 94px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Discovery/discovered-items-main-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 621px;height: 107px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI,Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Select the applicable asset record(s).

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI,Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="3. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Move to CMDB**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="4. ">

<span class="span_5">In the Confirmation window, click </span><span class="Strong_4" style="font-weight: bold;">Yes</span> (to move the asset) or **No** (to cancel the action). The discovered assets are moved to the CMDB.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="5. ">

<span class="span_5">In the Confirmation window, click </span><span class="Strong_4" style="font-weight: bold;">Continue</span> (to move the asset) or **Cancel** (to cancel the action). The discovered assets are moved to the CMDB.

</div>

# Change Risks

Use this function to define the risk factor of a change with customized color code.

In the main window, click **Admin > Change Management > Change Risk**. The Change Risk window displays.

New Change Risk

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Change Risk**. The New Change Risk window displays.

Enter a *Name* and *Description* for this category.

In the *Color* field, click in the field and choose a color from the picker OR type a 6-digit hex code.

Click **Add**.

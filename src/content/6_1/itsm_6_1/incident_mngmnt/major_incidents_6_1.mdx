# Major Incidents

Use this function to manage major incidents.

In the navigation pane, select **ITSM > Incident Management > Major Incidents**. The Major Incidents window displays.

New Major Incident

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Major Incident**.

Click **New**.

Complete the fields.

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

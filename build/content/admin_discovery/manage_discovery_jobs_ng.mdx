# Manage Discovery Jobs

Use this function to view, monitor, and manage active and completed discovery jobs.

In the main window, click **Admin > Discovery > Manage Discovery Jobs**. The Manage Discovery Jobs window displays.

## View Discovery Jobs

The window displays all discovery jobs with the following information:

- **Job Name**: Name of the discovery job
- **Status**: Current status (Running, Completed, Failed, Queued)
- **Start Time**: When the job started
- **End Time**: When the job completed
- **Duration**: Total time taken
- **Devices Discovered**: Number of devices found
- **Scan Profile**: The profile used for this discovery

## Job Actions

Select a job to perform the following actions:

**View Details**: Click a job line to view detailed information about the discovery process.

**Stop Job**: For running jobs, select the job and click **Stop Job** to halt the discovery process.

**Delete Job**: Select a completed or failed job and click **Delete** to remove it from the list.

**Export Results**: Select a job and click **Export** to download the discovery results.

## Job Details

When viewing job details, the following tabs are available:

- **Summary**: Overview of the discovery job
- **Devices**: List of discovered devices
- **Logs**: Detailed logs of the discovery process
- **Errors**: Any errors encountered during discovery

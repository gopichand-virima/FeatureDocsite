# Authentcation

The Authentication module secures access to the Virima Platform. It includes registration, login, password management, account lockout protection, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and Single Sign-On (SSO) integration.

This guide explains how to register, sign in, manage credentials, and configure authentication settings.

# User Authentication

## Register a new user

You can register a **New User** through the Virima registration process.

### Prerequisites

*   Organizational email address (e.g., `user@yourdomain.com`).
    
*   Your organization must be registered in the Virima system.
    
*   An administrator may restrict direct registration.
    

**Steps**

1.  Open the Virima Platform, and then click on **Go to Application.**
    
2.  On the Registration page, enter your organizational email address (e.g., `user@yourdomain.com`).
    
    *   If invalid, an error displays.
        
    *   If already registered, select **Sign In**.
        

![Registration.png](blob:https://virima.atlassian.net/1b508708-dc00-4ffe-8049-b25ad472348e#media-blob-url=true&id=9f1001e3-8590-4b2e-852b-6fec2e6296cc&collection=contentId-202801156&contextId=202801156&width=1658&height=873&alt=Registration.png)

4.  Complete the required fields in the registration form to create an account.
    
    *   First name
        
    *   Last name
        
    *   Email Address
        
    *   Password (must meet security requirements)
        
    *   Confirm password
        
5.  Select **Create account**.
    

![Create an account.png](blob:https://virima.atlassian.net/83f44ba7-61c6-460d-a88b-ff8ed48725bc#media-blob-url=true&id=3ec9b9a6-ac87-456d-9452-719d0d17a007&collection=contentId-202801156&contextId=202801156&width=1652&height=876&alt=Create%20an%20account.png)

6.  Check your inbox for a verification code.
    
7.  Enter the code and select **Verify Code**.
    

![Verify your email.png](blob:https://virima.atlassian.net/f84ed1e0-4797-4690-8d18-23b1f85b3383#media-blob-url=true&id=5c6cc431-79ee-4d30-ae35-9369a224d98f&collection=contentId-202801156&contextId=202801156&width=1646&height=862&alt=Verify%20your%20email.png)

8.  Your account activates when verification succeeds.
    
9.  The system displays a "Verification successful" message.
    

![Verification successfull.png](blob:https://virima.atlassian.net/246a85a8-8f97-4e20-9f1d-d1aad3f834e1#media-blob-url=true&id=2d717bc0-e291-4476-b6d1-8b3b517a2f63&collection=contentId-202801156&contextId=202801156&width=1655&height=874&alt=Verification%20successfull.png)

## Sign in

### Standard Sign in

1.  On the Registration page, click **Sign in.**
    

![Sign in.png](blob:https://virima.atlassian.net/3d488b5d-603a-41dc-9b0c-202cd0a0e311#media-blob-url=true&id=5f86aa96-54b7-4a98-9e41-d5555457c093&collection=contentId-202801156&contextId=202801156&width=1148&height=610&alt=Sign%20in.png)

2.  On the sign in page, enter your **Email** address and click **Continue**.
    

![sign in email.png](blob:https://virima.atlassian.net/a9941daf-29ac-40d5-ad82-c72c7d4ba5be#media-blob-url=true&id=db0832e1-0529-4bfb-b273-315b16f1544e&collection=contentId-202801156&contextId=202801156&width=1151&height=614&alt=sign%20in%20email.png)

3.  Enter the Password, and then Click **Login**.
    

![Login.png](blob:https://virima.atlassian.net/52c399a7-4c61-490a-b520-d532700a702c#media-blob-url=true&id=1f1b7a80-b5a9-43be-a5fb-82231f3d03ad&collection=contentId-202801156&contextId=202801156&width=1151&height=616&alt=Login.png)

4.  (Optional) Select **Remember me** to save the address.
    
5.  The system directs you to the Virima Application.
    

### Sign in with SSO (OIDC or SAML)

1.  On the Virima login page, select your authentication method:
    
    *   **Auth0 (OIDC)**
        
    *   **auth0Saml (SAML)**
        
2.  You are redirected to the identity provider login screen.
    

![Auth0 (OIDC).png](blob:https://virima.atlassian.net/9feae008-7ea3-4279-a12f-9639a5491ef2#media-blob-url=true&id=24bbb97a-9657-4525-95fb-44455567389d&collection=contentId-202801156&contextId=202801156&width=1147&height=619&alt=Auth0%20(OIDC).png)

3.  Enter your **Email address**.
    
4.  Enter your **Password**.
    
5.  Select **Continue**.
    
6.  (Optional) If enabled, choose an alternative login such as **Continue with Google**.
    
7.  After successful authentication, you are redirected back to the Virima Platform.
    

## Reset a Forgotten Password

1.  On the login to your account page, select **Forgot Your Password**.
    

![Forgot password.png](blob:https://virima.atlassian.net/a8b478ff-96cb-4db3-ad3d-846a5440c9c7#media-blob-url=true&id=4b61a966-db7e-4fdf-ad8b-5274af76c945&collection=contentId-202801156&contextId=202801156&width=1146&height=616&alt=Forgot%20password.png)

2.  To recover your account, Enter your email address.
    
3.  Check your inbox for a recovery code.
    
4.  Enter the code and select **Verify Code**.
    
5.  Enter the **New Password** and **Re-enter New Password** to confirm your new password.
    
6.  Select **Update Password.**

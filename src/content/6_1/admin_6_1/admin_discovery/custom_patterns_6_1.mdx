# Custom Patterns

The custom patterns provide the capability to add more known applications by providing Command and Arguments Details.

<div class="numlist1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

<span class="span_1">In the main window, select </span><span class="Strong_2" style="font-weight: bold;">Admin</span><span class="span_1" style="font-weight: bold;"> /> </span><span class="Strong_2" style="font-weight: bold;">Discovery</span><span class="span_1">** /> Custom Patterns**. The Patterns window displays. </span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/custom-patterns.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" style="width: 621px;height: 84px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

In the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose an action: **New Pattern**, <a href="../Common%20Topics/Delete-Remove.htm" target="_popup">Delete</a> or <a href="../Common%20Topics/Export.htm" target="_popup">Export</a>.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/pattern-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" style="width: 473px;height: 150px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the main window, click the **gear** icon and select **Discovery /> Custom Patterns**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/icon-custom-patterns.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

The Custom Patterns window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/custom-patterns-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 622px;height: 115px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Click **New Pattern**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/pattern-newUI.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 472px;height: 231px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

In the *Name* field, type a name for the pattern.

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

Under *Trigger Conditions*, in the *Command* and **Argument** fields, do any of the following:

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

Click the *Select Operator* drop-down list and choose the applicable operator.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

In the *Present Enter /[Command or Argument/]* field, enter the corresponding command or argument.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

To delete a command or argument, click the **X**.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

To delete a command or argument, click the **minus -**sign.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

To add a command or argument, click the **plus +** sign.

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

Under **Output**, do the following:

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

In the *CI Property* field, click the drop-down list and choose the applicable property.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" style="font-style: normal;">

In the *Command* field, enter the corresponding command.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

To delete a CI Property, click the **X**.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

To delete a command or argument, click the **minus -**sign.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

To add a CI property, click the **plus +** sign.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="8. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

# My Dashboard Contents

My Dashboard Contents provides detailed information about the data, widgets, and information displays available in your personal dashboard. Understanding these contents helps you make the most of your personalized workspace.

## Overview

My Dashboard Contents are automatically curated based on your user profile, role assignments, team memberships, and work patterns. The system intelligently surfaces relevant information to help you stay productive and informed throughout your workday.

## Content Categories

### Assignment-Based Content
Content derived from your current assignments:
- **Active Tickets**: All open tickets assigned to you
- **Pending Tasks**: Tasks requiring your action
- **Approvals Queue**: Items awaiting your approval decision
- **Escalations**: Items escalated to you for handling
- **Team Queue**: Shared team assignments you can pick up

### Activity-Based Content
Content based on your interactions:
- **Recently Viewed**: Records you recently accessed
- **Recently Modified**: Items you updated or changed
- **Recently Created**: Records you created
- **Watched Items**: Records you're following for updates
- **Bookmarked Items**: Items you've marked as favorites

### Role-Based Content
Content tailored to your organizational role:
- **Role-Specific Metrics**: KPIs relevant to your position
- **Department Dashboards**: Team or department views
- **Responsibility Areas**: Items in your areas of responsibility
- **Delegated Work**: Items delegated to you by others

### Time-Based Content
Content organized by timeframes:
- **Due Today**: Items with today's due date
- **Overdue Items**: Past due assignments
- **Due This Week**: Items due in next 7 days
- **Scheduled Activities**: Calendar of scheduled work
- **Upcoming Deadlines**: Future due dates and milestones

## Best Practices

1. **Regular Review**: Check My Dashboard at least twice daily
2. **Prioritize Critical**: Address red and orange items first
3. **Keep Clean**: Mark notifications as read, dismiss completed items
4. **Use Filters**: Focus on specific types of work when needed
5. **Update Progress**: Keep status and comments current
6. **Leverage Quick Actions**: Use inline actions to save time
7. **Watch Important Items**: Subscribe to critical records
8. **Review Metrics**: Monitor your performance and adjust as needed
9. **Optimize Layout**: Customize view to match your workflow
10. **Stay Informed**: Read notifications and activity updates

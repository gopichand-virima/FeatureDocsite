# Load Balancers

While viewing an asset record, click **Components**.

Click **Load Balancers**.

## Add a New Load Balancer

Click **Add**. The Load Balancer dialog box displays.

Click **New Load Balancer.**

Enter the applicable field information.

When all selections/entries are made, click **Save**.

## Modify Existing Load Balancer

Click **Existing Load Balancer**.

Enter the search criteria to locate the existing device.  *See also* [Advanced Search](/common_topics/advanced_search_6_1.mdx)*.*

Select the applicable device from the results list.

When all selections are made, click **Save**.

## Delete Load Balancer

While viewing the list of existing devices, select the device to delete, and click the **Delete** button.

# Change Timescale

Use this function to configure a resolution according to a priority basis.

In the main window, click **Admin /> Change Management /> Change Timescale**.

In the main window, click the **gear** icon, and select **Change Management /> Change Timescale**.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Change/icon-change-timescale.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

The Change Timescales window displays.

## Left Navigation Pane

The left navigation pane includes the following:

- [My Dashboard](/my_dashboard/my_dashboard_old_6_1.mdx) displays data content information with a customization option.
- [Discovery Scan](/discovery/about_discovery_scan_6_1.mdx) manages scanning activities (running, scheduling, etc.) and importing data (such as from AWS and Azure).
- [ITAM/ITSM](/itsm/config_mngmt/about_conf_mngt_6_1.mdx) enables functionality for configurable items (such as components) and establishes audit activities.


The menu heading displayed - either ITAM or ITSM - is based upon the subscription. - *ITAM* displays when CMDB and other modules (such as hardware assets and contract and vendor management) are enabled. - *ITSM* displays when CMDB, Incidents/Changes/Problems and other ITSM modules are enabled.


- [Vulnerability Management](/vulnerability_managment/vulnerability_management_6_1.mdx) identifies, evaluates, and reports security vulnerabilities in CIs present inside CMDB.
- [Reports](/reports/reports_6_1.mdx) generates both canned and custom ad hoc reports.

## Admin Window

The administrative functions include the following:

- [Organizational Details](/admin_org_details/about_org_details_6_1.mdx) specifies business-related details such as departments, cost centers, locations, designations, operational hours, and holidays.
- [Discovery](/admin_discovery/admin_discovery_6_1.mdx) configures clients, defines credentials, defines major software, manages processes, and other discovery-related functionality (scan configuration, monitoring profile, and application mapping).
- [SACM](/admin_sacm/admin_sacm_6_1.mdx) handles blueprints, relationship types, confidence configuration, and software-related functions.
- [Users](/msp/admin_users_6_1.mdx) creates users, roles, groups, time tracking reports and allows for AD import/authentication, and Azure AD and SAML configuration.
- [Incident Management](/itsm/incident_mngmt/about_incident_mngmnt_6_1.mdx) defines escalations, categories, error codes, the incident model, priority calculator, states/sub states, and timescale.
- [Request Management](/admin_request_mngmnt/about_request_mngmnt_6_1.mdx) ...
- [Change Management](/itsm/change_mngmnt/about_change_mngmnt_6_1.mdx) sets up categories, closure and error codes, the change model, priority calculator, risks/risk assessments, stage, state/state workflow, timescale, and types.
- [Problem Management](/itsm/problem_mngmt/about_problem_mngmnt_6_1.mdx) ...
- [Knowledge Management](/itsm/knowledge_mngmt/about_knowledge_mngmnt_6_1.mdx) ...
- [Release Management](/itsm/release_mngmt/about_release_mngmnt_6_1.mdx) ...
- [Project Management](/admin_project_mngmnt/about_project_mngmnt_6_1.mdx)...
- [Event Management](/admin_event_mngmnt/about_event_mngmnt_6_1.mdx)...
- [Integrations](/admin_integrations/about_integrations_6_1.mdx) provides a connection with Infoblox, Ivanti, and Cherwell and ServiceNow applications.
- [Others](/admin_other/admin_other_6_1.mdx) includes functions such as KPIs, Business Rules, Role Access and SMTP configuration.

# O<span class="inline-comment-marker">verview</span>

The <span class="Strong">Recent Scans</span> function provides IT teams with a centralized view of discovery scan activities across the infrastructure. It displays real-time and historical scan data, including status, duration, runtime, and completion details, through an easy-to-use dashboard. By offering transparency and traceability, this feature helps administrators monitor discovery processes and troubleshoot failures.

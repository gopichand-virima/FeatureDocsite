# View Resources

<span style="color: #dc143c;"><span style="color: #000000;">Use this function to view details for a user such as tasks performed, hours spent on the task, status of a task (such as </span>*Active*<span style="color: #000000;">, </span>*Started*<span style="color: #000000;">, and </span>*Not Started*</span>), and dates on which tasks occurred.

<div class="numlist1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, select **Admin /> Users /> Users**. The Access Management window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **View Resources**. A new window opens and provides the relevant details.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Users/user-view-resources.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 438px;height: 135px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

To toggle between Projects and Miscellaneous Task, use the drop-down list.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

To view users who have reported, click **Reported Users**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

To close the window, click the **X** in the right upper corner of the window.

</div>

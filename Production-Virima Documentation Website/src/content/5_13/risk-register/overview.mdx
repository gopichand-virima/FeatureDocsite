# Risk Register Overview

Risk Register provides comprehensive risk management capabilities to identify, assess, and mitigate risks across your IT environment.

## Key Features

- Risk identification and logging
- Risk assessment and scoring
- Mitigation planning
- Risk monitoring and tracking
- Compliance reporting

2. Assessing risk levels
3. Developing mitigation plans
4. Monitoring risk status

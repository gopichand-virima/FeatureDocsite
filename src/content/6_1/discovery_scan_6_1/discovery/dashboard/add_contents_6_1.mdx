# Adding Contents

1.  Click the <span class="Strong">Add Contents</span> button.

    <span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Discovery-Dashboard/Discovery%20Dashboard_1_8.png" original-image-title=""></span>

<!-- -->

2.  In the <span class="Strong">Search</span> window, locate and select available dashboard reports.

3.  If a text field appears:

    1.  Enter your search criteria and click the <span class="Strong">Search</span> icon.

    2.  Choose a report by clicking the radio button next to it or modify your criteria and search again.

4.  Choose where you want the report to appear on the dashboard. For example, selecting <span class="Strong">Add to the Top</span> will place the report above all existing content.

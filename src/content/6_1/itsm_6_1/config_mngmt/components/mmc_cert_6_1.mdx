# MMC Certificates

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

While viewing an asset record, click the **Components** option.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **MMC Certificates**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/mmc-certificates.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 476px;height: 129px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Select to view the details for either the *Current User* or the *Local Machine*.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Click the radio button next to each item (such as Trusted Publisher, Client Auth Issuer, and so forth) to view the relevant details for the selected certificate.

</div>

# Risk Register - Quick Start Guide

Start managing IT risks effectively.

## Prerequisites

- Risk Register module access
- Understanding of organizational risk tolerance
- Familiarity with risk management concepts
- Risk owner assignments

## Quick Start Steps

### Step 1: Configure Risk Framework
1. Navigate to Risk Register > Settings
2. Define risk categories (Strategic, Operational, Financial, Compliance)
3. Set risk probability levels (1-5)
4. Define impact levels (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
5. Configure risk tolerance thresholds

### Step 2: Create a Risk Entry
1. Click "New Risk"
2. Enter risk details:
   - Risk title and description
   - Category and subcategory
   - Risk owner
   - Affected assets/processes
3. Save the risk entry

### Step 3: Assess the Risk
1. Select the risk entry
2. Assess probability (1-5 scale)
3. Assess impact (Low to Critical)
4. System calculates risk score
5. Review risk heat map position

### Step 4: Create Mitigation Plan
1. Click "Add Mitigation Plan"
2. Define mitigation actions
3. Assign responsible parties
4. Set target completion dates
5. Track mitigation progress

# Move Assets to ServiceNow

<span class="span_5">Use this function to view the move assets to ServiceNow.</span>

|     |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
|:---:|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|     | An Asset record can also be moved from the <span style="background-color: #ffffff;">Details </span>window. However, wait until the scan is complete before initiating a move operation. Also, once a record is moved, it cannot be retrieved. |

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the navigation pane, select <span class="Strong_4" style="font-weight: bold;">Discovery Scan</span><span class="span_5" style="font-weight: bold;"> /> </span><span class="Strong_4" style="font-weight: bold;">Discovered Items</span>. The Discovered Items window displays.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Select the applicable asset record(s).

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="3. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, select **Move to ServiceNow** **Copy to Service Now.**

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

<span class="span_5">In the Confirmation window, click </span><span class="Strong_4" style="font-weight: bold;" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">Yes</span>**Continue** (to complete the move) or **No** **Cancel** (to cancel the action).

</div>

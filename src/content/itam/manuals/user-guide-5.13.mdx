# IT Asset Management - User Manual

Complete guide for IT asset lifecycle management in Virima 5.13.

## Asset Lifecycle

### Lifecycle Stages
1. **Requested**: Asset procurement initiated
2. **Ordered**: Purchase order created
3. **Received**: Asset delivered
4. **In Stock**: Available for deployment
5. **Deployed**: In active use
6. **In Repair**: Under maintenance
7. **Retired**: End of life
8. **Disposed**: Properly disposed/recycled

## Hardware Asset Management

### Asset Types
- Workstations (desktops, laptops)
- Servers (physical, virtual)
- Network equipment
- Mobile devices
- Peripherals
- Data center equipment

### Asset Tracking
- Barcode/QR code scanning
- RFID tracking
- GPS location (for mobile assets)
- Check-in/check-out system

## Software Asset Management

### License Types
- Per-device licenses
- Per-user licenses
- Concurrent/floating licenses
- Subscription-based (SaaS)
- Enterprise agreements

### License Compliance
- Track installations vs. entitlements
- Identify over-licensed software
- Detect unauthorized installations
- Generate compliance reports

## Financial Management

### Cost Tracking
- Purchase cost
- Maintenance costs
- Support costs
- Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)

### Depreciation
Supported methods:
- Straight-line depreciation
- Declining balance
- Custom depreciation schedules

## Best Practices

- Maintain accurate asset records
- Regular physical inventory audits
- Proactive contract renewal management
- Software license optimization
- Proper asset disposal procedures
- Integration with CMDB for complete visibility

Use this function to create ITSM records.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.Omit" madcap:autonum="1. ">

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.Omit" madcap:autonum="2. ">

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.Omit" madcap:autonum="3. ">

</div>

</div>

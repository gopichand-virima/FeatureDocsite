# About the Virima Modules and Functions

The All Modules window contains quick access to selected functions. From this main window, all functions are reached either through:

- TheÂ **Navigation pane** (down the left side of the window)
- The **Admin menu** (in the upper right corner of the window)

## Home Page/All Modules Window

The home page provides quick access to the most frequently used modules, such as favorite functions and dashboards.

- **Adding** a Favorite. Locate an icon in the applicable section. Then, click and drag/drop the icon into the Favorite section inside the box with the text, "Drag here to add."
- **Deleting** a Favorite. Locate the icon in the Favorites section. Then, click and drag the icon above the Favorite section until the text "Drag here to delete" inside a red box displays.

## Left Navigation Pane

The left navigation pane includes the following:

- [My Dashboard](/my_dashboard/my_dashboard_both_6_1.mdx) displays data content information with a customization option.
- [CMDB](/cmdb_left_nav/cmdb_overview_6_1.mdx) provides a centralized repository for Configuration Items (CIs) and their relationships, with detailed CI profiles (attributes, ownership, linked components, dependencies, audit history, and lifecycle).
- [Discovery Scan](/discovery/about_discovery_scan_6_1.mdx) manages scanning activities (running, scheduling, etc.) and importing data (such as from AWS and Azure).
- [ITAM](/itam/about_itam_6_1.mdx) (asset management functions) and [ITSM](/itsm/about_itsm_6_1.mdx) (service management functions)


The menu heading displayed - either ITAM or ITSM - is based upon the subscription.


- [Vulnerability Management](/vulnerability_managment/vulnerability_management_6_1.mdx) identifies, evaluates, and reports security vulnerabilities in CIs present inside the CMDB.
- [Self Service](/self_service/about_self_service_6_1.mdx) creates catalog items and shows incidents and requests for the current user.
- [Project and Program Management](/prog_proj_mngmnt/about_prog_proj_mngmnt_6_1.mdx) defines project aspects and managing related projects.
- [Risk Register](/risk_register/about_risk_register_6_1.mdx) provides a tool for managing risk and compliance.
- [Reports](/reports/reports_6_1.mdx) generates both canned and custom ad hoc reports.
- **[MSP](/msp/about_msp_6_1.mdx)** displays a list of clients and includes a Dashboard, Reports, My Incidents, and Administrative functions (Client, Users, Mailbox Monitoring Configuration, and Email Templates). It also provides access to the Virima application.

## Admin Window

The administrative functions include the following:

- [Organizational Details](/admin_org_details/about_org_details_6_1.mdx) specifies business-related details, such as departments, cost centers, locations, designations, operational hours, and holidays.
- [Discovery](/admin_discovery/admin_discovery_6_1.mdx) configures clients, defines credentials and major software, manages processes, and other discovery-related functionality (scan configuration, monitoring profile, and application mapping).
- [SACM](/admin_sacm/admin_sacm_6_1.mdx) handles blueprints, relationship types, confidence configuration, and software-related functions.
- [Users](/msp/admin_users_6_1.mdx) creates users, roles, groups, and time tracking reports. Also allows for AD import and authentication, Azure AD and SAML configuration.
- [Management Functions](/admin/admin_mngmnt_functions_6_1.mdx) for [Changes](/itsm/change_mngmnt/about_change_mngmnt_6_1.mdx), [Contracts](/admin_contract_mngmt/about_contract_mngmnt_6_1.mdx), [Events](/admin_event_mngmnt/about_event_mngmnt_6_1.mdx), [Hardware Assets](/admin_hardware_asset_mngmnt/about_hw_asset_mngmnt_6_1.mdx), [Incidents](/itsm/incident_mngmt/about_incident_mngmnt_6_1.mdx), [Knowledge](/itsm/knowledge_mngmt/about_knowledge_mngmnt_6_1.mdx), [Problems](/itsm/problem_mngmt/about_problem_mngmnt_6_1.mdx), [Projects](/admin_project_mngmnt/about_project_management_6_1.mdx), [Releases](/itsm/release_mngmt/about_release_mngmnt_6_1.mdx), and [Requests](/admin_request_mngmnt/about_request_mngmnt_6_1.mdx).
- [Integrations](/admin_integrations/about_integrations_6_1.mdx) provides credential and mapping information for Cherwell, Ivanti, ServiceNow, and Jira Credentials and Infoblox configuration.
- [Others](/admin_other/admin_other_6_1.mdx) includes functions such as Announcements, KPIs, Business Rules, Role Access, and SMTP configuration.

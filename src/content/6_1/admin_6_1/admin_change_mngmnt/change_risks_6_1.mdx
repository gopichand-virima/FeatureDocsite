# Change Risks

Use this function to define the risk factor of a change with customized color code.

In the main window, click **Admin /> Change Management /> Change Risk**. The Change Risk window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Change/change-risk-main-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

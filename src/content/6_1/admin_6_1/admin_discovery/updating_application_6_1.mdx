# Updating the Application

1. In the Web App, go to **Admin** \> **Discovery** \> **Client**.

2. Click the **Download** icon next to your **Client ID** to fetch updates.![Updating the Application](../Resources/Images/Admin - Discovery/Updating the Application.png)

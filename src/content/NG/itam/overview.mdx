# ITAM Overview

IT Asset Management (ITAM) provides comprehensive tracking and management of hardware and software assets throughout their lifecycle.

## Key Features

- Asset inventory management
- License compliance tracking
- Software asset management
- Hardware lifecycle management
- Cost optimization
- AI-powered cost analysis

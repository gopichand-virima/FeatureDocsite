# Operational Hours

Use this function to define working or company's operational timings, and then save the same in the application.

 --- -----------------------------------------------------
   Operational hours cannot be deleted, only modified.
 --- -----------------------------------------------------

In the main window, click **Admin \> Organization Details \> Operational Hours**. The Work Schedules window displays.

![arrow border](../Resources/Images/arrow-border.PNG)

[[[Closed]{.image .placeholder .MCDropDown_Image_Icon original-image-src="../../Skins/Default/Stylesheets/Images/transparent.gif" original-image-title="" height="12" width="16" Operational Hours](javascript:void(0)){.MCDropDownHotSpot .dropDownHotspot .MCDropDownHotSpot_ .MCHotSpotImage aria-expanded="false"}]{.MCDropDownHead .dropDownHead}

 -- ----- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1. To schedule round-the-clock hours, click **Round the clock (24 hours)**, then select the days of the week for which the setting applies.
 -- ----- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 2. To schedule specific hours, click **Select Operational Hours**. Then, select the days of the week, and specify the start and end times.
 -- ----- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: Ind50
![select op hours old 326x102](../Resources/Images/Admin - Org Details/select-op-hours-old_326x102.png)
[[[Closed]{.image .placeholder .MCDropDown_Image_Icon original-image-src="../../Skins/Default/Stylesheets/Images/transparent.gif" original-image-title="" height="12" width="16" Operational Hours](javascript:void(0)){.MCDropDownHotSpot .dropDownHotspot .MCDropDownHotSpot_ .MCHotSpotImage aria-expanded="false"}]{.MCDropDownHead .dropDownHead}

 --- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Not all functions include an option to edit record line items shown in the main window.
 --- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- -------------------------------------------------------
 1. Navigate to the window containing the record to edit.
 -- ----- -------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 2. Click the line containing the record. The applicable window or dialog box displays.
 -- ----- -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- -----------------------------------
 3. Make the necessary modifications.
 -- ----- -----------------------------------

 -- ----- -------------------------------------------
 4. Click **Save** or **Add**, as applicable.
 -- ----- -------------------------------------------

When editing an existing item, other options may be available, such as adding [Tasks](/itsm/release_mngmt/tasks_6_1.mdx){.MCTopicPopup .MCTopicPopupHotSpot .a}, [Comments](/discovery/comments_6_1.mdx){.MCTopicPopup .MCTopicPopupHotSpot .a}, and [Attachments](/discovery/attachments_6_1.mdx){.MCTopicPopup .MCTopicPopupHotSpot .a} and viewing [History](/common_topics/history_6_1.mdx){.MCTopicPopup .MCTopicPopupHotSpot .a}.

 --- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   The options change based upon the function; therefore, not every option may be shown.

# Department Members

Use this function to add new department members for the organization.

In the main window, click **Admin > Organization Details > Departments**. The Departments window displays.

Click a record in the list for the department to which you want to add a new member.

In the *Details* window, click the **Members** tab.

Click on **New User**, search and select the member.

Click on **Unlink** to dissociate the member.

Click on **Export** to export the selected member details.

! New Member

Click **New User**.

In the Members dialog box, search for and select the users to add as members to this department.

When all selections are made, click **Save**.

Click the **Members** tab.

Click the checkbox and select the member to dissociate.

In the confirmation window, click **Unlink**.

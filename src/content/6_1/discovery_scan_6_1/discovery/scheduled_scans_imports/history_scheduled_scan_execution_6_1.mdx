# History of Scheduled Scan Execution

- In Scheduled Scans and Imports page

- Click on the schedule ID.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Scheduled-Scans-and-Imports/Scheduled%20Scans%20and%20Imports_1_6.png" original-image-title=""></span>

- Will open the schedule in edit mode

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Scheduled-Scans-and-Imports/Scheduled%20Scans%20and%20Imports_1_7.png" original-image-title=""></span>

- Displays the details of the scheduled job

- Click on <span class="Strong">Related Scans</span> tab.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Scheduled-Scans-and-Imports/Scheduled%20Scans%20and%20Imports_1_8.png" original-image-title=""></span>

- A row is displayed for every scan execution.

- Displayed information

  - Fields populated while scheduling a Scan

    - Id, Name, Location, Client, HostName, Probe.

  - Fields providing details of the run

    - <span class="Strong">Status</span>: Indicates success or failure of the Scan

    - <span class="Strong">Run By</span>: Login details of scheduled scan

    - <span class="Strong">Triggered At</span> - Timestamp of scan start

    - <span class="Strong">Completed At</span> - Timestamp when scan completed

    - <span class="Strong">Duration </span>- Total time from scan start to finish in

- Use column filters to find a particular scan.

- Click on <span class="Strong">Scan ID</span> or any other field to see details of the scan.

  - Upon Click user is redirected to <span class="Strong">Recent Scans</span> Page with details of the scan.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Scheduled-Scans-and-Imports/Scheduled%20Scans%20and%20Imports_1_9.png" original-image-title=""></span>

- Refer to <span class="Strong">Recent Scans</span> Page document to understand the details on this Page

- <span class="Strong">Previous Trigger</span> – last time this schedule fired (after your first run).

- <span class="Strong">Next Trigger</span> – when it will fire next (updates as soon as you save or change timezone/wheels).

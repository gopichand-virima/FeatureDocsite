# Change CI Category

Use this function to configure change associated CI categories.

In the main window, click **Admin /> Change Management /> Change CI Category**. The Change CI Category window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Change/ci-category-main-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

In the main window, click the **gear** icon and select **Change Management /> Change CI Category**.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Change/icon-change-ci-category.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

The Change CI Category window displays.

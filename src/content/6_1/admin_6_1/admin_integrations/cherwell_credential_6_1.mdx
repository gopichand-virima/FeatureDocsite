# Cherwell Credential

Use this function to specify credential details for Cherwell.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, click **Admin /> Integrations /> Cherwell Credential**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/cherwell-credential-new-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter the *Username* for this credential.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Click **Change Password** and enter a new password.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

To view the password, click the **eye** icon.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

To cancel the password change operation, click the **x** icon.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Enter the *URL* for the domain.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Enter the *API Key* to use for the domain.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

<span style="color: #dc143c;"><span style="color: #000000;">Select to enable the ability to integrate with the application.</span></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Save**.

</div>

# Knowledge Articles

Use this function to view and search the published knowledge articles (knowledge topic except FAQs). The published Knowledge articles cannot be edited: an RFC must be created to edit an article

In the main window, select **ITSM > Knowledge Management > Knowledge Articles**.

!

!


### Searching

To search for an existing article, use one of two locations:

- The *Search* field across the top of the window does a global search, which searches the entered keyword in title of Incidents, Problems, Changes, Requests and Knowledges. Blueprint name and IP address can be used as a keyword to search for CI’s.
- The *Search* field adjacent the Article Details. This only searches for articles with a configured *Type* as "Text."


### Comments

- To view comments about an article, click on the article in the list, and click the **Comments** tab.
- To add a comment:
- Click **New Comment**.
- Enter the comment text.
- To add an attachment, click **Attach** and then search for and attach the applicable document
- Click **Add**.


### Viewing

To view an article, click on the article in the list. The article displays and contains details about the article including the user rating, owner details, and article information. The article information displayed is based upon the type of article: *Text*, *Link* or *Attachment*.

Other actions include: *Rate this Article*, *Subscribe*, or *Mark as Important*.

!

Click each of the respective links.

- For *Rate this Article*, indicate a Yes or No as to the helpfulness, the number of stars (1 through 5), and Suggestions and click **Save**.
- For *Subscribe Me*, when clicked, the logged in user is automatically subscribed. When subscribed, the user will be notified about any changes that occur for this article.
- For *Mark as Important*, enter a Reason and click **Save**. This activity is updated under the *Importance of Knowledge*.


### Questions

To enter a question regarding the published article:

- From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Have a Question**? The Ask your Question dialog box displays.

!

- In the *Question* field, type the question search criteria. Then click **Search**.


### FAQs

To view the frequently asked questions and corresponding answer, from the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose FAQs. The FAQs window displays a list of the most common questions. *Refer to [FAQs](/itsm/knowledge_mngmt/faqs_6_1.mdx).*


### Expired Articles

When the *Valid to Date* has been reached, an email is sent to the owner of the knowledge. To change the duration, the Owner clicks **Request for Edit**.

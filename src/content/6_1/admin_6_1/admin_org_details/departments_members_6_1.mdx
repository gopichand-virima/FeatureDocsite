# Department Members

Use this function to add new department members for the organization.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, click **Admin /> Organization Details /> Departments**. The Departments window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Org%20Details/depart-old.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 629px;height: 92px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click a record in the list for the department to which you want to add a new member.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the *Details* window, click the **Members** tab.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Click on **New User**, search and select the member.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Click on **Unlink** to dissociate the member.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

Click on **Export** to export the selected member details.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Org%20Details/Department_Member_6.1.png" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

New Member

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **New User**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

In the Members dialog box, search for and select the users to add as members to this department.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

When all selections are made, click **Save**.

</div>

Edit Member

For Email Preferences and [Inbox](../Admin%20-%20Users/Inbox.htm), refer to [Admin /> Users](../Admin%20-%20Users/Users.htm).

Unlink Member

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click the **Members** tab.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click the checkbox and select the member to dissociate.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the confirmation window, click **Unlink**.

</div>

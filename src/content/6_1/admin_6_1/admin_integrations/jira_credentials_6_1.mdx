# Jira Credentials

Use this function to specify the credentials for the Jira domain.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, click **Admin /> Integrations /> Jira Credentials**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/JIra-Credentials-6.1.png" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter a *Username*, the applicable *Domain* and *API Key*.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Click **Next**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Choose one or both:

</div>

<div style="text-indent: 0.5in;">

*Asset Management* – Sync Virima metadata into Jira Assets (CMDB).

</div>

<div style="text-indent: 0.5in;">

*Service Management* – Sync Jira Incidents and Changes into Virima.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Click **Next**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

**Asset management setup:**

</div>

<div style="text-indent: 0.5in;">

Choose specific object schemas from the dropdown or select *All Object Schemas* to include all available schemas.

</div>

<div style="text-indent: 0.5in;">

Select **Yes** to sync Virima Blueprints into Jira schemas. **No** to skip.

</div>

<div style="text-indent: 0.5in;">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/JIra-Credentials-Asset_Management_6.1.png" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

**Service management setup:**

</div>

<div style="text-indent: 0.5in;">

Select *All Projects* or choose specific projects from the dropdown. Click **Next**.

</div>

<div style="text-indent: 0.5in;">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/Jira_Service_Management_6.1png.png" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="8. ">

Select *All incident type issues* or choose specific types.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="9. ">

Select *Incident type issues*, choose how to retrieve issues by clicking **Assets** or **Custom** (link Asset Name to Jira incidents or map properties), and click **Next**.<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/Jira_incident.png" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="10. ">

Select *All change type issues* or choose specific types.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="11. ">

Select *Change types issues*, choose how to retrieve change issues by clicking **Assets** or **Custom** (link Asset Name to Jira changes or map properties), and click **Save**.<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/Jira_change.png" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="12. ">

A popup will confirm the process. You will receive an email notification once it finishes. Select **Yes** to confirm.

</div>

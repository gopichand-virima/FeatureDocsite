# Delete Report

Use this function to delete a report from the applicable entities (for example, My Dashboard) and archive the report.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

From the navigation pane, select either **Reports /> Ad Hoc Reports** or **Reports /> Canned Reports.** The Reports window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

In the list, select one or more reports to delete.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="3. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Delete**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Click on the **bin** icon to delete the report.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="5. ">

In the Confirmation Message window, click **Yes**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="6. ">

In the Confirmation Message window, click **Continue**.

</div>

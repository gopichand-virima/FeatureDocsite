# Deleting a Scan Schedule

1.  Tick the checkbox(es) for the job(s) to remove.

2.  Click <span class="Strong">Select Actions </span>→<span class="Strong"> Delete</span>.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Scheduled-Scans-and-Imports/Scheduled%20Scans%20and%20Imports_1_15.png" original-image-title=""></span>

3.  In the <span class="Strong">Delete Confirmation</span> dialog, type <span class="Strong">Delete </span>exactly, then click <span class="Strong">Delete</span>.

    <span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Scheduled-Scans-and-Imports/Scheduled%20Scans%20and%20Imports_1_16.png" original-image-title=""></span>

4.  No scans will be executed from the point of delete

5.  If an active scan is currently running, The scan will complete.

6.  Scan details or its history will not be accessible

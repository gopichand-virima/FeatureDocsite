# Article Helpful Threshold

Use this function to rate the published articles in the application, so the article can be updated based upon the rating.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, click **Admin /> Knowledge Management /> Article Helpful Threshold**. The Article Helpful Threshold dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/article-helpful-threshold-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, click the **gear** icon and select **Knowledge Management /> Article Helpful Threshold**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/icon-article-helpful-threshold-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

The Article Helpful Threshold dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Knowledge/article-helpful-threshold-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter the number of times the article was rated, and the number of times the article was reported as not helpful.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Click **Save**.

</div>

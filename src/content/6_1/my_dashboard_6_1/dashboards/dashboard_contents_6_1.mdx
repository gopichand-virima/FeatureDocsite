# Dashboard Contents

The Discovery Scan Dashboard can be configured to show a variety of reports. The reports can be added, removed, and placed in various locations on the Dashboard. Each report has options for generating or saving the report.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the navigation pane, select **Discovery Scan /> Dashboard**. The Dashboard window displays.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Discovery/discovery-scan-dashboard-new-main.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

C<span class="span_1">lick the </span><span class="span_2" style="font-weight: bold;">Add Contents</span><span class="span_1"> button. The Search window displays</span>.

</div>

<div class="Ind50 step-media" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="span_1"><span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Discovery/add-contents-search-window.PNG" original-image-title="" style="border-left-style: solid;border-left-width: 1px;border-left-color: #808080;border-right-style: solid;border-right-width: 1px;border-right-color: #808080;border-top-style: solid;border-top-width: 1px;border-top-color: #808080;border-bottom-style: solid;border-bottom-width: 1px;border-bottom-color: #808080;" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI"></span>  
</span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50 step-media">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Discovery/add-contents-search-window-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the *Search For* field, click the drop-down list and choose the desired report.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Click the drop-down list and choose the desired report.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI,Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="5. ">

If a text field displays, do the following:

</div>

<div class="NumLista" madcap:autonum="a. ">

Enter the search criteria in the field and click the **Search** icon. A list of results displays.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="b. ">

Enter the keyword and click the desired file names next to the radio buttons displayed.

</div>

<div class="NumListb" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="c. ">

Either click the radio button next to the desired report OR enter different criteria and search again.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

Select a **location** for the report to display on the dashboard. For example, "Add to the Top" will place the report at the top of the Dashboard above all other reports currently shown.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

Click the **Add Now** button to view the report.

</div>

|     |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
|-----|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|     | Once you place a report on the dashboard, it cannot be moved. To change the location, remove the report, select it again, and then specify a new location. Also, some reports can only be added to the top or bottom of the report area. This is due to the size of the report. |

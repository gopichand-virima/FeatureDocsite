# Department Members

Use this function to add new department members for the organization.

- In the main window, click **Admin > Organization Details > Departments**. The Departments window displays.

!

- Click a record in the list for the department to which you want to add a new member.
- In the *Details* window, click the **Members** tab.
- Click on **New User**, search and select the member.
- Click on **Unlink** to dissociate the member.
- Click on **Export** to export the selected member details.

!

!


### New Member

- Click **New User**.
- In the Members dialog box, search for and select the users to add as members to this department.
- When all selections are made, click **Save**.


### Edit Member

Not all functions include an option to edit record line items shown in the main window.


- Navigate to the window containing the record to edit.
- Click the line containing the record. The applicable window or dialog box displays.
- Make the necessary modifications.
- Click **Save** or **Add**, as applicable.

When editing an existing item, other options may be available, such as adding [Tasks](/itsm/release_mngmt/tasks_6_1.mdx), [Comments](/discovery/comments_6_1.mdx), and [Attachments](/discovery/attachments_6_1.mdx) and viewing [History](/common_topics/history_6_1.mdx).


The options change based upon the function; therefore, not every option may be shown.


For Email Preferences and [Inbox](/admin_users/inbox_6_1.mdx), refer to [Admin > Users](/admin_users/users_6_1.mdx).


### Unlink Member

- Click the **Members** tab.
- Click the checkbox and select the member to dissociate.
- In the confirmation window, click **Unlink**.

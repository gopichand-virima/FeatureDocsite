# Edit Stockroom

This feature enables editing the configured values of the existing contract, updating, and then saving the same in the application.

Click to open required Stockroom. The Stockroom page displays.

The Stockroom displays the following information:

- Details
- Tasks
- Comments
- Attachment
- History

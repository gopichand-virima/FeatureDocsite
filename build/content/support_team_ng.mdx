# Support Team

Use this function to configure and manage support teams, groups, and their assignments.

Support teams organize technicians into logical groups based on skills, location, function, or service areas, enabling efficient ticket routing and workload management.

In the main window, click **Admin > Service Desk > Support Team**. The Support Team window displays.

## New Support Team

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Support Team**. The New Support Team window displays.

Enter a *Team Name* and *Description*.

Configure the team settings:

### Basic Information

**Team Type**:

- Application Support
- Desktop Support
- Network Operations
- Help Desk
- Field Services
- Database Administration
- Security Operations
- Custom

**Location**: Select the primary location for this team.

**Manager**: Select the team manager or supervisor.

**Email**: Team email address for notifications.

**Phone**: Team contact number.

### Team Members

Click **Add Members** to add technicians to this team.

For each team member, configure:

**User**: Select the team member.

**Role**: Assign team role
- Team Lead
- Senior Technician
- Technician
- Junior Technician
- Specialist

**Primary Team**: Check if this is the user's primary team assignment.

**Skills**: Tag specific skills for this member
- Windows Administration
- Network Troubleshooting
- Application Support
- Hardware Repair
- Database Management
- Security
- etc.

**Availability**:

- Full-time
- Part-time
- On-call only
- Backup support

**Assignment Method**:

For this team, configure how tickets are assigned:

- **Manual Assignment**: Tickets wait in queue for manual assignment
- **Round-Robin**: Distribute tickets evenly among members
- **Least Busy**: Assign to member with fewest open tickets
- **Skill-Based**: Match ticket requirements to member skills
- **Workload-Based**: Consider ticket complexity and estimated effort

Click **Add** to save the team member.

### Team Schedule

Configure when this team is available.

Click the **Schedule** tab.

**Business Hours**: Select the business hours calendar for this team.

**On-Call Schedule**: Configure on-call rotation if applicable.

**Holidays**: Specify team-specific holidays.

**Shift Coverage**: Define shift schedules if 24/7 support
- First Shift: 8 AM - 4 PM
- Second Shift: 4 PM - 12 AM
- Third Shift: 12 AM - 8 AM

### Team Capabilities

Click the **Capabilities** tab.

Define what this team can handle:

**Categories**: Select ticket categories this team supports.

**Priority Levels**: Specify if limited to certain priorities.

**Service Areas**: Define supported services or applications.

**Geographic Coverage**: Areas this team supports.

**Supported CIs**: CI types this team can service.

### Service Level Targets

Click the **SLA Targets** tab.

Configure team-specific SLA targets:

**Response Time Targets** (by priority):

- P1: 15 minutes
- P2: 1 hour
- P3: 4 hours
- P4: 1 business day
- P5: 2 business days

**Resolution Time Targets** (by priority):

- P1: 4 hours
- P2: 8 hours
- P3: 2 business days
- P4: 5 business days
- P5: 10 business days

**Target Metrics**:

- First Contact Resolution Rate: X%
- Customer Satisfaction Target: X%
- SLA Compliance Rate: X%

When all configurations are complete, click **Save**.

## Edit Support Team

Navigate to the window containing the team to edit.

Click the line containing the team. The team details window displays.

Make modifications to:

- Team information
- Team members
- Schedule
- Capabilities
- SLA targets

Click **Save**.

## Team Performance Dashboard

To view team performance metrics:

Select a support team.

Click **View Team Dashboard**.

The dashboard displays:

**Current Workload**:

- Open tickets assigned to team
- Tickets by priority
- Tickets by age
- Average response time
- Average resolution time

**Team Performance**:

- SLA compliance rate
- First contact resolution rate
- Customer satisfaction score
- Ticket volume trends

**Team Members**:

- Active tickets per member
- Individual performance metrics
- Availability status
- Current assignments

**Trends**:

- Week over week comparison
- Month over month comparison
- Seasonal trends

## Team Workload Management

To view and manage team workload:

Select a support team.

Click **Manage Workload**.

**Queue View**:

- Unassigned tickets in team queue
- Pending tickets
- Tickets awaiting response

**Actions**:

- **Assign Tickets**: Manually assign from queue
- **Reassign**: Move tickets between team members
- **Request Assistance**: Get help from other teams
- **Escalate**: Escalate to senior team or management

## Team Calendar

View team schedules and availability:

Select a support team.

Click **View Calendar**.

The calendar shows:

- Team member shifts
- On-call rotations
- Vacation and time off
- Training sessions
- Team meetings
- Peak workload periods

## Team Skills Matrix

Maintain a skills matrix for the team:

Select a support team.

Click **Skills Matrix**.

The matrix displays:

- Team members (rows)
- Skills and competencies (columns)
- Proficiency levels (1-5, beginner to expert)

**Actions**:

- **Update Skills**: Modify member skills and proficiency
- **Identify Gaps**: See skill gaps in the team
- **Training Needs**: Identify training requirements
- **Cross-Training**: Plan cross-training initiatives

## Clone Support Team

To create a new team based on an existing one:

Select a team.

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Clone Team**.

Enter a new name and make modifications as needed.

Optionally copy:

- Team members
- Schedule
- Capabilities
- SLA targets

Click **Add**.

## Deactivate/Activate Team

To deactivate a team:

Select the team.

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Deactivate**.

Deactivated teams:

- No longer appear in assignment options
- Cannot receive new tickets
- Existing tickets remain assigned
- Can be reactivated later

To reactivate:

Select a deactivated team.

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Activate**.

## Delete Support Team

Ensure no active tickets are assigned to this team before deletion.

Click the line that contains the team to delete.

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Delete**.

All tickets assigned to this team must be reassigned before deletion can complete.

## Team Communication

Configure team communication channels:

Select a support team.

Click **Communication Settings**.

**Team Email**: Shared team inbox.

**Chat Channel**: Link to team chat (Slack, Teams, etc.).

**Notification Preferences**:

- New ticket notifications
- Escalation alerts
- SLA breach warnings
- Team announcements

Click **Save**.

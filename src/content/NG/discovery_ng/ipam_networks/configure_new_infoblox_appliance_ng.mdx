# Configure New Infoblox Appliance

While viewing the Infoblox Appliances window, click the **New Infoblox Appliance** icon. The New Infoblox Appliance window displays.

**Name**. Enter a name for the InfoBlox Appliance.

**IP Address**. Enter the IP address for the InfoBlox Appliance.

**Credential**. Click the drop-down list and select a credential.


If the credential needed is not in the list, click the **New** button next to the dropdown. The [Add Credential](/admin_discovery/credentials_new_ng.mdx) window displays. Complete the credential fields and click **Add**.


When all selections are made, click **Save & Close**. The window closes and refreshes and shows the newly created Infoblox Appliance.

In the Infoblox Appliances window, click the **Sync Inventory** button.

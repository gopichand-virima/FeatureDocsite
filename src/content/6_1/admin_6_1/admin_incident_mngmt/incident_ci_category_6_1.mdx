# Incident CI Category

Use this function to configure incident associated CI categories.

In the main window, click **Admin > Incident Management > Incident CI Category**. The Incident CI Category window displays.

## New Incident CI Category

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Incident CI Category**. The New Incident CI Category window displays.

Enter a *Name* and *Description*.

To add an image, click **Upload**, search for and select an image, then click **OK**.

Click **Add**.

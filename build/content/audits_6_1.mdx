# Audits

Use this feature to analyze the product or its process to assess compliance with defined specifications or standards of the deliverable.

In the navigation pane, select **ITSM > Configuration Management > Audits**. The Audits window displays.

New Audit Process

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Audit**.

Click **New**.

Complete the fields, referring to the information below.

- **Name**, Enter a for this audit process.
- **Description**. Enter information that details this audit process.
- **Auditor**. Click **Add,** then search for and select the applicable user(s).
- **Checked By**. Click **Add**, then search for and select the applicable user(s).
- **Run a Scan**. Select this option to trigger a validation type probe scan on a device. Based on the result of the scan, the audit is marked as passed or failed.
- **Audit Result**. Click the drop-down list and select the applicable result, such as **Pass**, **Fail** or **In Progress**.
- **Configuration Items**. Click **Add**, then search for and select the applicable configuration items for this audit process.

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

# Monitoring Profile: Notifications

Use this function to send a report to the configured users, roles, groups, or departments.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the Notification section, click **Add**. The *Send Report To dialog box* displays. By default, the *Users* tab is selected.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/dmp-send-report-user.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/dmp-send-report-user-new.png" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

For the **Users**, **Roles**, **Groups**, **Departments** or **From Record** tabs, do the following (and a selection on every tab is not required):

</div>

<div class="NumLista" madcap:autonum="a. ">

Search for the applicable user, role, group department or record by typing characters in the *Search* field.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="b. ">

If a user, role, group, department or record is found, a list of results displays.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="c. ">

Click in the selection box. The dialog box refreshes and the user, role, group department or record is added.

</div>

<div class="Ind75">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/dmp-send-report-user-add.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind75">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/dmp-send-report-user-add-new.png" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Repeat the process to add more users, roles, groups, departments or records.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="4. ">

When all selections are made, click **Save**. The selected information is added to the Notifications section.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="5. ">

When all selections are made, click **Add**. The selected information is added to the Notifications section.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/discovery-mon-prof-notifications.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/discovery-mon-prof-notifications-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="border-left-style: solid;border-left-width: 1px;border-left-color: ;border-right-style: solid;border-right-width: 1px;border-right-color: ;border-top-style: solid;border-top-width: 1px;border-top-color: ;border-bottom-style: solid;border-bottom-width: 1px;border-bottom-color: ;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="6. ">

To update any of the notification selections, click **Update**, make the applicable changes, and click **Save**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="7. ">

To update any of the notification selections, click **+**plus sign, make the applicable changes, and click **Save**.

</div>

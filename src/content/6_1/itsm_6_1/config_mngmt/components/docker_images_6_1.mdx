## Add a New Docker Image

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **Add**. The Docker Images dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/docker-images-add-new.png" original-image-title="" style="width: 371px;height: 213px;"></span>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **New Docker Image.**

</div>

<div class="NumList2" style="font-style: normal;" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter the applicable field information.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

# Business Services

Use this function to create and manage business services.

In the navigation pane, select **ITSM > Service Portfolio > Business Services**. The Business Services window displays.

New Business Service

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Business Service**.

Click **New**.

Complete the fields.

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

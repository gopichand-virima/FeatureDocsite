# New BSM View

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, select **Admin /> SACM /> BSM View**. The BSM View window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20SACM/bsm-view-old.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 620px;height: 103px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New BSM View**. The BSM View window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20SACM/Custom_BSM_views_6.1.png" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter a *Name* for the BSM view.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

From the *Include Relationships* and *Include Bluerpints* drop-down lists, select the type of required relationship and blueprint.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

From the *Exclude Relationships* and *Exclude Blueprints* drop-down lists, select the type of relationship and blueprint to be omitted.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

Check *Include External CI Communication* option to include external communications to the corresponding CI.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

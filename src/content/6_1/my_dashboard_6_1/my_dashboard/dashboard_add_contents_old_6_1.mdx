# Add Contents to My Dashboard

Additional content can be added to the dashboard. Examples include Ad Hoc Reports, Calendars (change or outage), KPIs, etc.

Depending on the type of content added, various other functions become available. For example, if an Ad Hoc Report is added to the dashboard, a set of icons displays. These provide options to perform actions, such as displaying the entire report, exporting or saving the report, and minimizing or maximizing this section on the dashboard.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/ITSM/add-content-icons.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

To place more content on the dashboard, do the following:

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the navigation page, click **My Dashboard**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **Add Contents**. The Search dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/ITSM/add-contents-search.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 418px;height: 283px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the *Search For* field, click the drop-down list and choose the desired item.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="4. ">

In the blank *Search* field, enter the search criteria and click the **Search** icon. A list of results displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Either click the radio button next to the desired item OR enter different criteria and search again.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

After making a selection, choose a **location** for the item to display on the dashboard, such as top, bottom, left, or right.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

**Example 1:**  **Add to the Top** will place the selection (e.g., *Average Initial Response Time*) at the top of the Dashboard above all other items except the main dashboard area.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/ITSM/add-contents-example.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 622px;height: 176px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

**Example 2:** **Add to the Right** will place the new selection (e.g., *Change Efficiency rate*) to the right of the top item.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/ITSM/add-contents-example-two-across.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 622px;height: 92px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

You can also drag and drop items on the dashboard. In the example below, *Change Efficiency rate* was moved from the right to the left and above *Average Initial Response Time*. In addition, *All Incidents by Location* was added to the dashboard.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/ITSM/add-contents-reorg.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 622px;height: 204px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

Repeat the above process to place additional content on the dashboard.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="8. ">

To remove an item from the dashboard (excluding the top panel), click the **trashcan** icon.

</div>

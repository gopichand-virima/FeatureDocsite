# Risk Register Overview

Risk Register provides comprehensive risk management capabilities to identify, assess, and mitigate risks across your IT environment.

## Key Features

- Risk identification and logging
- Risk assessment and scoring
- Mitigation planning
- Risk monitoring and tracking
- Compliance reporting
- AI-powered risk prediction

## Getting Started

Learn how to manage risks effectively:
1. Creating risk entries
2. Assessing risk levels
3. Developing mitigation plans
4. Monitoring risk status
5. Utilizing predictive risk analytics

## Version Information

This documentation applies to Virima **NextGen**. This version includes enhanced features and AI-powered capabilities not available in previous versions.

# Business Hours

Use this function to configure business hours calendars that define when your service desk operates.

Business hours are used to calculate SLA timeframes, schedule automated tasks, and inform users of support availability. You can create multiple business hours calendars for different locations, time zones, or support tiers.

In the main window, click **Admin > Service Desk > Business Hours**. The Business Hours window displays.

## New Business Hours Calendar

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Business Hours**. The New Business Hours window displays.

Enter a *Name* and *Description* for this calendar.

Select the **Time Zone** for this calendar.

### Configure Daily Hours

For each day of the week, configure:

**Working Day**: Check if this is a working day.

**Hours**: Define the operating hours for this day.

To add multiple shifts for a single day:

- Click **Add Shift**
- Enter start time and end time for each shift
- Example: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM

**Closed**: Check if the service desk is closed on this day.

### Configure Holidays

Click the **Holidays** tab.

Click **Add Holiday**.

Enter the following information:

- **Holiday Name**: Name of the holiday
- **Date**: Specific date or recurring date
- **Recurring**: Check if this holiday recurs annually
- **Closed All Day**: Check if the service desk is completely closed

Click **Add** to save the holiday.

### Configure Exceptions

Click the **Exceptions** tab to define one-time schedule changes.

Click **Add Exception**.

Enter:

- **Date**: Specific date for the exception
- **Hours**: Modified operating hours
- **Reason**: Description of the exception

Click **Add** to save the exception.

When all configurations are complete, click **Save**.

## Common Business Hours Configurations

Examples of business hours calendars you might create:

**Standard Business Hours**:
- Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Saturday - Sunday: Closed

**Extended Support**:
- Monday - Friday: 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed

**24/7 Support**:
- All days: 24 hours

**Follow-the-Sun**:
- Different calendars for different global regions
- Ensures continuous coverage across time zones

## Edit Business Hours

Navigate to the window containing the calendar to edit.

Click the line containing the calendar. The calendar details window displays.

Make the necessary modifications to:

- Daily hours
- Holidays
- Exceptions

Click **Save**.

## Set as Default

To set a calendar as the default for the organization:

Select the calendar.

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Set as Default**.

The default calendar will be used for SLA calculations unless a specific calendar is assigned.

## Clone Business Hours

To create a new calendar based on an existing one:

Select a calendar.

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Clone Calendar**.

Enter a new name and make modifications as needed.

Click **Add**.

## Delete Business Hours

Ensure no SLAs or other configurations are using this calendar before deletion.

Click the line that contains the calendar to delete.

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Delete**.

## Import/Export Holidays

To import a list of holidays:

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Import Holidays**.

Select the file format (CSV, iCal).

Upload the file with holiday data.

Click **Import**.

To export holidays:

Select a calendar.

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **Export Holidays**.

Select the desired format.

Click **Export**.

# Incident States and SubStates

Use this function to add new state defining status for an Incident being handled by an Assignee.

In the main window, click **Admin /> Incident Management /> States and Sub States**. The States and Sub States window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Incident/incident-state-main-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

In the main window, click the **gear** icon and select **Incident Management /> States and Sub States**.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Incident/icon-states-substates.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

The States and Sub States window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Incident/incident-states-main-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

New Incident States and Sub States

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Incident State**. The Incident States and Sub States window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Incident/incident-state-new-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **New**.The Change Stage window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Incident/incident-state-new-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter a *Name* and *Description*.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the *State* field, click the drop-down list and select the order/sequence.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

In the *SLA Phase* and *SLA Action* fields, click the drop-down lists and select the applicable phase and action.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

In the *Mandatory Details* field, click **Add**the **plus + sign** then search for and select the details required for the state.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

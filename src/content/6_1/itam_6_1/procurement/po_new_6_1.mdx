# New Purchase Order

Use this feature to configure the details of the purchase of products or services.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, select **ITAM /> Procurement /> Purchase Order**. The Purchase Order window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, select **New Purchase Order**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the information section, enter the following: 

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/ITAM/PO-new-old-info.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 474px;height: 147px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Purchase Order No**. Type the purchase order number. This is a required field.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Purchase Order Date**. Click the **calendar icon** and select the date.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Vendor**. Enter the name of the vendor.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Assigned To**. Click **Add** and select the person to whom the purpose order is assigned.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Ship To**. Click **Add** and enter the address where the item is to be shipped.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Bill To Location**. Click **Add** and enter the address where the bill is to be sent.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Status**. Click the drop-down list and select a status for the purchase order--for example, Ordered, Received, and so forth.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Description**. Enter information about this purchase order.

</div>

<div class="emdash1" madcap:autonum="- ">

 

</div>

General

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Select type required shipping from Shipping dropdown.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Select terms of shipping payment from Terms dropdown.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter total shipping price in the Ship Rate box.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Enter the total cost of shipping and billing in the Total Cost box.

</div>

Details

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Enter the name of the user-initiated.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter the name of the contract in the Contract name box.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

By default logged in user name displays in the Requested box.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Click Add to update the corresponding Department.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Enter the budget for the requested PO.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

Enter vendor account details in the Vendor Account box.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

Enter<span style="color: #ff0000;">/This step is incomplete in WIKI</span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="8. ">

Click Requested Date text box to select purchase order date.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="9. ">

Click Expected Delivery Date text box to select a date to deliver the product.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="10. ">

Click Ordered On text box to select the date on which the product/service is being ordered.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="11. ">

Click Add to update the corresponding PO.

</div>

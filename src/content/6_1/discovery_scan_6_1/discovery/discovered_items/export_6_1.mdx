# <span class="Strong">Export</span>

- Select one or more checkboxes.

- Click on <span class="Strong">Select Actions </span>and then Select <span class="Strong">Export.</span>

- A pop up confirmation message shall be displayed as below,

  - Click on <span class="Strong">Continue </span>to export the selected records.

  - Click on <span class="Strong">Cancel </span>to abort export operation.

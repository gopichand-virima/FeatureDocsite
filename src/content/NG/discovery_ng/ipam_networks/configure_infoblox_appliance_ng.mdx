# To configure Infoblox Appliance to work with Virima

1. Login to the Infoblox and Select the **Data Management** option from the top menu.

2. Under the **Data Management** tab, select the **DHCP** option.

3. Select the **Grid DHCP Properties** option from the left side menu of **DHCP** tab.

4. Click on the **Fingerprint** filter tab.

5. Select the **Enable Device Identification** option.

6. A new set of options appears as shown in Fig below.

1. Click on the **Enable DHCP Fingerprint** option.

2. Click on the **Enable DHCP Lease History** option.

3. Click the **Save and Close** button.

4. Click the **Restart** **DHCP Service** icon on the toolbar (looks like a blue play button).

1. Click **OK** when the information window displays.

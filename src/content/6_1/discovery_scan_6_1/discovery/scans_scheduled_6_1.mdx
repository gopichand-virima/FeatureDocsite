# Scheduled Scans

Use this function to enable the execution of scan functionality according to a configured date and time.

In the navigation pane, select **Discovery Scan /> Scheduled Scan**. The Scheduled Scans window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Discovery/scheduled-scan-main-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI"></span>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

</div>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

</div>

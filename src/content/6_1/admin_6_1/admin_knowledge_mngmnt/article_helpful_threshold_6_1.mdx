# Article Helpful Threshold

Use this function to rate the published articles in the application, so the article can be updated based upon the rating.

 -- ----- ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1. In the main window, click **Admin \> Knowledge Management \> Article Helpful Threshold**. The Article Helpful Threshold dialog box displays.
 -- ----- ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: {.Ind50
![article helpful threshold old](../Resources/Images/Admin - Knowledge/article-helpful-threshold-old.PNG)

 -- ----- -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 2. Enter the number of times the article was rated, and the number of times the article was reported as not helpful.
 -- ----- -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- -----------------
 3. Click **Save**.
 -- ----- -----------------

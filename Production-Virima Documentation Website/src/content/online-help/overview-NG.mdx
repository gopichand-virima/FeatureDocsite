# Welcome to Virima Documentation

Virima is a comprehensive IT Service Management and Discovery platform designed to help organizations 
manage their IT infrastructure, assets, and services with precision and efficiency.

## Key Features

### Service Mapping
Automatically discover and map your IT infrastructure and business services with real-time visibility.

### Asset Management
Track and manage all your IT assets throughout their lifecycle with comprehensive CMDB capabilities.

### Discovery & Monitoring
Continuous discovery of hardware, software, and cloud resources across your entire IT estate.

### Compliance & Security
Ensure compliance and maintain security posture with detailed reporting and audit capabilities.

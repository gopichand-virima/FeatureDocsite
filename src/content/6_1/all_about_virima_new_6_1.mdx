## Left Navigation Pane

The left navigation pane includes the following:

<div class="Bullet1">

[My Dashboard](My%20Dashboard/My-Dashboard-Old.htm) displays data content information with a customization option.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Discovery Scan](Discovery/About-Discovery-Scan.htm) manages scanning activities (running, scheduling, etc.) and importing data (such as from AWS and Azure).

</div>

<div class="Bullet1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

[ITAM/ITSM](ITSM/Config%20Mngmt/About-Conf-Mngt.htm) enables functionality for configurable items (such as components) and establishes audit activities.

</div>

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Reports](Reports/Reports.htm) generates both canned and custom ad hoc reports.

</div>

## Admin Window

The administrative functions include the following:

<div class="Bullet1">

[Organizational Details](Admin%20-%20Org%20Details/About-Org-Details.htm) specifies business-related details such as departments, cost centers, locations, designations, operational hours, and holidays.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Discovery](Admin%20-%20Discovery/Admin-Discovery.htm) configures clients, defines credentials, defines major software, manages processes, and other discovery-related functionality (scan configuration, monitoring profile, and application mapping).

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[SACM](Admin%20-%20SACM/Admin-SACM.htm) handles blueprints, relationship types, confidence configuration, and software-related functions.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Users](Admin%20-%20Users/Admin-Users.htm) creates users, roles, groups, time tracking reports and allows for AD import/authentication, and Azure AD and SAML configuration.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Incident Management](Admin%20-%20Incident%20Mngmnt/About-Incident-Mngmnt.htm) defines escalations, categories, error codes, the incident model, priority calculator, states/sub states, and timescale.

</div>

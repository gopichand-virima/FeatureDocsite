# Risk Register - User Manual

Complete guide for IT risk management in Virima 5.13.

## Risk Management Framework

### Risk Categories
- **Strategic Risks**: Business direction and objectives
- **Operational Risks**: Day-to-day operations and processes
- **Financial Risks**: Budget, costs, and financial losses
- **Compliance Risks**: Regulatory and legal requirements
- **Technology Risks**: IT infrastructure and systems
- **Security Risks**: Cybersecurity and data protection

## Risk Assessment

### Probability Assessment
| Level | Description | Likelihood |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| 1 - Rare | Unlikely to occur | < 10% |
| 2 - Unlikely | Could occur | 10-30% |
| 3 - Possible | Might occur | 30-50% |
| 4 - Likely | Will probably occur | 50-70% |
| 5 - Almost Certain | Expected to occur | > 70% |

### Impact Assessment
| Level | Description | Business Impact |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| Low | Minimal impact | < $10K loss |
| Medium | Moderate impact | $10K-$100K loss |
| High | Significant impact | $100K-$1M loss |
| Critical | Severe impact | > $1M loss |

### Risk Score Calculation
Risk Score = Probability Ã— Impact

## Risk Treatment Strategies

### Four T's of Risk Management
1. **Tolerate**: Accept the risk (within risk appetite)
2. **Treat**: Implement controls to mitigate
3. **Transfer**: Insurance or outsourcing
4. **Terminate**: Eliminate the risk source

## Risk Monitoring

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Number of active risks by category
- Average time to mitigate risks
- Risk trend analysis
- Compliance adherence rate
- Control effectiveness ratings

### Risk Reviews
- Monthly operational risk reviews
- Quarterly strategic risk reviews
- Annual comprehensive risk assessments
- Ad-hoc reviews for emerging risks

## Compliance Management

### Regulatory Frameworks
- SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley)
- GDPR (Data Protection)
- HIPAA (Healthcare)
- PCI DSS (Payment Cards)
- ISO 27001 (Information Security)

## Best Practices

- Regular risk identification workshops
- Maintain comprehensive risk documentation
- Assign clear risk ownership
- Monitor risk indicators continuously
- Update risk assessments regularly
- Link risks to business objectives
- Foster risk-aware culture
- Document lessons learned

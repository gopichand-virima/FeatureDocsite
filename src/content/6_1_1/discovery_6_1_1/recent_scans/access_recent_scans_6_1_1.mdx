# Accessing Recent Scans

In the navigation pane, select **Discovery Scan > Recent Scans**. The Recent Scans window displays.

The Recent Scans page provides a comprehensive list of all discovery scan activities, showing both completed and in-progress scans. This centralized view enables administrators to monitor scan execution, review results, and troubleshoot any issues that may have occurred during the discovery process.

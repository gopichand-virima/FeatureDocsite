# Department Members

Use this function to add new department members for the organization.

 -- ----- --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1. In the main window, click **Admin \> Organization Details \> Departments**. The Departments window displays.
 -- ----- --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[[[Closed]{.image .placeholder .MCDropDown_Image_Icon original-image-src="../../Skins/Default/Stylesheets/Images/transparent.gif" original-image-title="" height="12" width="16" Member](javascript:void(0)){.MCDropDownHotSpot .dropDownHotspot .MCDropDownHotSpot_ .MCHotSpotImage aria-expanded="false"}]{.MCDropDownHead .dropDownHead}

 -- ----- ---------------------
 1. Click **New User**.
 -- ----- ---------------------

 -- ----- --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 2. In the Members dialog box, search for and select the users to add as members to this department.
 -- ----- --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- -----------------------------------------------
 3. When all selections are made, click **Save**.
 -- ----- -----------------------------------------------

[[[Closed]{.image .placeholder .MCDropDown_Image_Icon original-image-src="../../Skins/Default/Stylesheets/Images/transparent.gif" original-image-title="" height="12" width="16" Member](javascript:void(0)){.MCDropDownHotSpot .dropDownHotspot .MCDropDownHotSpot_ .MCHotSpotImage aria-expanded="false"}]{.MCDropDownHead .dropDownHead}

 --- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Not all functions include an option to edit record line items shown in the main window.
 --- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- -------------------------------------------------------
 1. Navigate to the window containing the record to edit.
 -- ----- -------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 2. Click the line containing the record. The applicable window or dialog box displays.
 -- ----- -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- -----------------------------------
 3. Make the necessary modifications.
 -- ----- -----------------------------------

 -- ----- -------------------------------------------
 4. Click **Save** or **Add**, as applicable.
 -- ----- -------------------------------------------

When editing an existing item, other options may be available, such as adding [Tasks](/itsm/release_mngmt/tasks_6_1.mdx){.MCTopicPopup .MCTopicPopupHotSpot .a}, [Comments](/discovery/comments_6_1.mdx){.MCTopicPopup .MCTopicPopupHotSpot .a}, and [Attachments](/discovery/attachments_6_1.mdx){.MCTopicPopup .MCTopicPopupHotSpot .a} and viewing [History](/common_topics/history_6_1.mdx){.MCTopicPopup .MCTopicPopupHotSpot .a}.

 --- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   The options change based upon the function; therefore, not every option may be shown.
 --- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

For Email Preferences and [Inbox](/admin_users/inbox_6_1.mdx), refer to [Admin \> Users](/admin_users/users_6_1.mdx).

[[[Closed]{.image .placeholder .MCDropDown_Image_Icon original-image-src="../../Skins/Default/Stylesheets/Images/transparent.gif" original-image-title="" height="12" width="16" Member](javascript:void(0)){.MCDropDownHotSpot .dropDownHotspot .MCDropDownHotSpot_ .MCHotSpotImage aria-expanded="false"}]{.MCDropDownHead .dropDownHead}

 -- ----- ----------------------------
 1. Click the **Members** tab.
 -- ----- ----------------------------

 -- ----- ---------------------------------------------------------
 2. Click the checkbox and select the member to dissociate.
 -- ----- ---------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- -----------------------------------------------
 3. In the confirmation window, click **Unlink**.
 -- ----- -----------------------------------------------

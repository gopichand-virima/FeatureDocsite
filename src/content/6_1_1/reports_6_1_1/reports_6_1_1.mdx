# About Reports

Use this function to generate ad-hoc reports based on a time schedule by defining a query based on various parameters. These Ad Hoc reports can be saved, becoming canned reports, and published on the Dashboard or imported into a spreadsheet.

This module includes the following functions:

- [Ad Hoc Reports](/reports/reports_ad_hoc_6_1_1.mdx)
- [Canned Reports](/reports/reports_canned_6_1_1.mdx)
- [Dashboard](/dashboards/dashboard_reports_actions_6_1_1.mdx)

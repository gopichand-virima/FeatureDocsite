# History of Scheduled Scan Execution

- In Scheduled Scans and Imports page

- Click on the schedule ID.

![Scheduled Scans and Imports 1 6](../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Scheduled-Scans-and-Imports/Scheduled Scans and Imports_1_6.png)

- Will open the schedule in edit mode

![Scheduled Scans and Imports 1 7](../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Scheduled-Scans-and-Imports/Scheduled Scans and Imports_1_7.png)

- Displays the details of the scheduled job

- Click on **Related Scans** tab.

![Scheduled Scans and Imports 1 8](../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Scheduled-Scans-and-Imports/Scheduled Scans and Imports_1_8.png)

- A row is displayed for every scan execution.

- Displayed information

 - Fields populated while scheduling a Scan

 - Id, Name, Location, Client, HostName, Probe.

 - Fields providing details of the run

 - **Status**: Indicates success or failure of the Scan

 - **Run By**: Login details of scheduled scan

 - **Triggered At** - Timestamp of scan start

 - **Completed At** - Timestamp when scan completed

 - **Duration **- Total time from scan start to finish in

- Use column filters to find a particular scan.

- Click on **Scan ID** or any other field to see details of the scan.

 - Upon Click user is redirected to **Recent Scans** Page with details of the scan.

![Scheduled Scans and Imports 1 9](../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Scheduled-Scans-and-Imports/Scheduled Scans and Imports_1_9.png)

- Refer to **Recent Scans** Page document to understand the details on this Page

- **Previous Trigger** -- last time this schedule fired (after your first run).

- **Next Trigger** -- when it will fire next (updates as soon as you save or change timezone/wheels).

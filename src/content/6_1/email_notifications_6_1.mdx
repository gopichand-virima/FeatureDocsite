# Email Notifications

Various functions in the application will generate an email notification when certain actions take place.

The user receives a notification based upon their email preferences as specified in their [user](Admin%20-%20Users/Users.htm) account. (Admin /> Users).

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Users/email-preferences.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

## Example Functions

The list of functions that generate an email notification is too long to list in this topic. However, below are some examples:

<div class="Bullet1">

When [creating a request](ITSM/Request-Fulfillment/Requests.htm) or [creating an incident](ITSM/Incident-Mngmt/Incidents.htm), an email notification is sent to Requester, Assignee, and to users based upon their email preferences.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

When adding a [new CIÂ component](ITSM/Config%20Mngmt/Config%20Items/Config-Item-New.htm), an email notification is sent to the applicable Roles as per the SACM RACI matrix and email preferences.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

When creating or updating a [project](Prog-Proj%20Mngmnt/Projects.htm), an email notification is sent to Project Manager(s) associated with the project.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

When an [SLA](Admin%20-%20Other/SLA.htm) breach occurs, a notification email is sent to Change Manager(s) and CAB members.

</div>

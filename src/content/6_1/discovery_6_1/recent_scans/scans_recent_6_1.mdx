# Recent Scans

Use this function to view all scans that have been performed.

In the navigation pane, select **Discovery Scan > Recent Scans**. The Recent Scans window displays.

The window lists all recently executed scans with their status, timing information, and results. Users can click on any scan to view detailed information including:

- Scan configuration details
- Discovered assets
- Scan logs and messages
- Error information (if applicable)
- Related tasks and comments

Edit Recent Scans

Select a recent scan to view or manage its details.

Available options include:
- View scan details and configuration
- Review discovered items from this scan
- Access scan logs and troubleshooting information
- Add tasks or comments related to the scan
- Export scan results

Delete Recent Scans

Recent scan records cannot be deleted as they provide important audit trail information for compliance and troubleshooting purposes.

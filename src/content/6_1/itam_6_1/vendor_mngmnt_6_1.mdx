# Vendor Management

Use this function to manage business information related to suppliers and service providers for the company.

In the main window, select **ITAM /> Vendor Management**. The Vendor Management window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/ITAM/vendor-mngmnt-main-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/ITAM/vendor-mngmnt-main-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

New Vendor

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Vendor**. The New Vendor window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **New**. The New Vendor window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Complete the fields, referring to the information below.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

### Product Models

Use this function to view and add product models for this vendor.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, select a Vendor.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

In the Details window, click **Product Models**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

To add a new product model, click **New Product Models**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Enter the *Name*, *Model Number* and *Model Categories*.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

When all entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

### Catalog Items

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, select a Vendor.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click the **Catalog Items** tab. A list of all catalog items associated with this vendor displays. This information is view only.

</div>

### Contract

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, select a Vendor.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click the **Contact** tab. A list of all contracts between the vendor and the company displays. This information is view only.

</div>

# Subnets

While viewing an asset record, click **Components**.

Click **Subnets**.

## Add New Subnet

Click **Add**. The Subnet dialog box displays.

Click **New Subnet** (if not already selected).

Enter the applicable information in each field.

When all entries are made, click **Save**.

## Modify Existing Subnet

Click **Existing Subnet**.

Enter the search criteria to locate the existing device. *See also [Advanced Search](/common_topics/advanced_search_5_13.mdx).*

Select the applicable device from the results list.

When all selections/entries are made, click **Save**.

## Delete Subnet

While viewing the list of existing subnets, select the device to delete, and click the **Delete** button.

# Cherwell New Mapping

Use this function to enter the details for mapping properties between Virima and Cherwell.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, click the **gear** icon and select **Integrations /> Cherwell Mappings**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/icon-cherwell.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

The Cherwell Mappings window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/cherwell-mappings.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 623px;height: 116px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

To create a new mapping, click **New Mapping**. The New Cherwell Mapping window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/new-cherwell-mapping-new.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 624px;height: 193px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the *Virima Blueprint* field, click the drop-down list and select the applicable blueprint.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

In the *Cherwall Object* field, click the drop-down list and select the applicable object.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

When the selections are made, click **Add**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

In the *Virima Property* field, click the drop-down list and select the property to map to the Cherwell Propery.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

In the *Cherwell Property* field, click the drop-down list and select the property to map to the Virima Property.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="8. ">

In the Value field, type the value that applies to both properties.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="9. ">

<span style="color: #dc143c;"><span style="color: #000000;">To use a correlator, select </span></span>**Correlator**<span style="color: #dc143c;"><span style="color: #000000;">.</span></span><span style="color: #000000;"> </span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="10. ">

To remove a mapping, select the item in the list of mappings and click the **trashcan icon**.

</div>

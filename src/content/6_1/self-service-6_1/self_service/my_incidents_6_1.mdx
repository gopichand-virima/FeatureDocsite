# My Incidents

Use this function to display the status of Incidents that are created by the logged in user.

<table class="TableStyle-Note" style="mc-table-style: url(&#39;../Resources/TableStyles/Note.css&#39;);margin-left: 0;margin-right: auto;width: 100%;" data-cellspacing="0">

<tr class="TableStyle-Note-Body-Body1">
<td class="TableStyle-Note-BodyB-Column1-Body1"> </td>
<td class="TableStyle-Note-BodyA-Column2-Body1"><div class="Bullet1">
Role access for incidents is required.
</div>
<div class="Bullet1">
An incident can be canceled, but this must be done before the incident is closed.
</div></td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

In the navigation pane, select **Self Service /> My Incidents**. The resulting window displays a list of all incidents.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Self-Service/my-incidents-list-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

To view the related incident information, under Incident ID, click the **hyperlink**. The related [Incident](../ITSM/Incident-Mngmt/Incidents.htm) window displays.

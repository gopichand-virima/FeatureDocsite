# Exporting Records Without Selecting Any Record

1. **Open the Export Wizard**

 - Click on **Select Actions** and select **Export** from the dropdown menu.

 - The system will display the Export wizard.

 ![Discovered Items 1 7](../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Discovered-Items/Discovered Items_1_7.png)

2. **Select Blueprints**

 - In the Blueprints step, use the search bar to find and tick the blueprint(s) you want to export (e.g., AWS, Windows, etc.).

 - Click **Next** to proceed.

3. **Select Components**

 - If the selected blueprints have component types, choose the components you need.

 - Click **Next** to continue.

 ![Discovered Items 1 8](../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Discovered-Items/Discovered Items_1_8.png)

4. **Select Properties**

 - Start with **Main Properties**.

 - If you see the message: "No main properties are found for selected blueprints," switch to **Other Properties**.

 - In **Other Properties**, search for and check the fields you want to include (move them to **Selected Items** if required by your UI).

 - Click **Next** or proceed as per the UI.

 ![Discovered Items 1 9](../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Discovered-Items/Discovered Items_1_9.png)

5. **Export the Data**

 - Click **Export**.

 - A dialog will appear with the message:\
 *"The complete CMDB information will be generated to an Excel spreadsheet. You will receive an email when it is ready to be downloaded."*

 - Click **Continue** to confirm.

6. **Download the Exported File**

 - Wait for the email or notification.

 - Follow the link provided in the email/notification to download the Excel file.

# Known Errors

Use this function to view and create known errors.

In the main window, select **ITSM /> Problem Management /> Known Error Database /> Known Errors**.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/ITSM/Problem%20Management/known-errors-main-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/ITSM/Problem%20Management/known-errors-main-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

New Known Error

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Known Error**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **New**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Complete the fields, referring to the table below.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

# About the Virima Application

This applies to v5 only.

A summary of the Virima features and benefits is shown below.

::: {.Bullet1
**Agentless Discovery**. Collects valuable data of IP-addressable assets without agents, and automatically populates the inventory catalog with hundreds of OS attributes and device-specific hardware details.

::: {.Bullet1
**CMDB**. A centralized database comprising hardware and software assets and the relationships between them, commonly referred to as Configuration Items (CIs).

::: {.Bullet1
**Application Dependency Mapping**. Identifies applications running in the IT ecosystem and masp the dependencies between them providing clear visibility of the environmental health and overall application performance.

::: {.Bullet1
**ViVID Service Mapping**. Equips the IT department with real-time representations of the relationships between your IT infrastructure, application dependencies, business services, and ITSM processes, thus helping your staff to instantly point out where outages occur and their impacts.

::: {.Bullet1
**Windows Discovery Agent**. Provides functionality for when a persistent agent is preferred. Installing this agent on servers and PCs provides constant monitoring of configuration changes, and allows for software metering.

::: {.Bullet1
**ITSM**. IT Service Management involves designing, creating, delivering, supporting, and managing the lifecycle of IT services. This enables the delivery of IT to the rest of the organization as a service, extending reach within the organization and enables collaboration.

::: {.Bullet1
**ITOM**. Keeps an organization\'s IT infrastructure managed and well-maintained to ensure that the delivery of IT services is up to standard for running your business. ITOM Essentials can be delivered as an all-in-one solution or integrated with many popular ITSM platforms eliminating disruptions.

::: {.Bullet1
**ITAM**. Ensures an organization\'s assets are categorized, well-maintained, upgraded on time, and disposed of when required, assuring easy IT portfolio governance by providing a holistic view of all your IT assets.

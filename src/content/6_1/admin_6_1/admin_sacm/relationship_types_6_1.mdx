# Relationship Types

Use this function to define the relationship between a source and target.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, click **Admin /> SACM /> Relationship Types**. The Relationship Types window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20SACM/relationship-types-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

New Relationship Type

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose an action: **New Relationship Type.**

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20SACM/relationship-types-add-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter the *Primary Name* to define the relationship from the Source to the Target.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter the *Reverse Name* to define the relationship from the Target to the Source.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Select a *Color* to represent the relationship between the Source and the Target and vice versa.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Check if the *UserType Relation* should be applied.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

Select a **Line Stroke**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

Click **Add**.

</div>

</div>

</div>

</div>

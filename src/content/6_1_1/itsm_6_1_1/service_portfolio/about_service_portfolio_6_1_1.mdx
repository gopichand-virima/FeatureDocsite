# About Service Portfolio

Use this function to manage the complete set of services managed by a service provider.

The Service Portfolio represents the complete set of services that are managed by a service provider, including services in development (pipeline), active services (catalog), and retired services.

The Service Portfolio includes:

- Service Pipeline - services in development
- Service Catalog - active services available to customers
- Retired Services - services no longer offered

# Discovery Scan Dashboard

The Dashboard contains a list of all the recent scans and any reports that have been added to the window.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the navigation pane<span class="span_1">, select **Discovery Scan /> Dashboard**</span>. The Dashboard displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Discovery/example-dashboard-layout.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 547px;height: 351px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

To add more information to the window, click [Add Contents](Dashboard/Add-Contents.htm).

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

To see all records, click **View All**. The [Discovered Items](Discovered-Items.htm) window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="4. ">

To view the details for a specific record, click the line item in the Recent scans list. The applicable [Discovered Items](Discovered-Items.htm) window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="5. ">

To change the information displayed on the dashboard, see [Customize the Discovery Scan Dashboard](Dashboard/Dashboard-Customization.htm).

</div>

# About Knowledge Management

The Knowledge Management module provides comprehensive tools for creating, organizing, and maintaining a knowledge base. This section covers the administrative configuration options available for knowledge management.

Knowledge Management in Virima enables organizations to:

- Create and maintain knowledge articles
- Organize content into categories and folders
- Control article lifecycle and approval workflows
- Enable self-service through knowledge base
- Track article usage and effectiveness
- Manage article ratings and feedback
- Configure search and discovery
- Maintain version control and history

## Key Configuration Areas

The following administrative functions are available for Knowledge Management:

- **Article Templates**: Create reusable article formats
- **Knowledge Categories**: Organize articles into categories
- **Approval Workflows**: Configure article review and approval
- **Article Lifecycle**: Manage article states and transitions
- **Access Control**: Define who can view and edit articles
- **Article Ratings**: Enable user feedback and ratings
- **Search Configuration**: Optimize knowledge search
- **Analytics**: Track article performance and usage

Each of these areas can be configured to match your organization's knowledge management strategy.

## Knowledge Base Structure

**Article Organization**:

- Categories and subcategories
- Tags and labels
- Related articles
- Article hierarchies

**Article Types**:

- How-to guides
- Troubleshooting articles
- FAQ entries
- Best practices
- Policy documents
- Reference materials

## Article Lifecycle

**Draft**: Work in progress

**Review**: Submitted for approval

**Published**: Live and searchable

**Retired**: No longer active but retained

**Archived**: Historical record

# Branding

The **Branding** feature in Virima allows administrators to customize the look and feel of authentication pages with their organization’s identity. By applying a primary color, uploading a company logo, and configuring a custom URL, organizations can ensure a consistent brand experience for all users logging into the platform.

# Prerequisites

*   Administrator access to the Virima Platform.
    
*   Organization logo in square or rectangular format.
    
*   Logo file size must not exceed **15 KB**.
    
*   Valid URL for your authentication page.
    

# Configure Branding

Use this procedure to customize the branding of your organization’s authentication pages.

1.  In the left navigation pane, under **Setup**, select **Branding**.
    

![Branding.png](blob:https://virima.atlassian.net/a08549b3-ce8d-4af9-b545-bb29ddc48c5c#media-blob-url=true&id=42c819ba-57b3-43e5-a6ca-84a734c3ab1c&collection=contentId-204472364&contextId=204472364&width=1144&height=614&alt=Branding.png)

2.  Under **Choose a primary color**, select a color option that matches your organization’s theme.
    
3.  In the **URL** field, enter the custom URL for your authentication page.
    
    *   Example: `xyz.example.com`
        
4.  Under **Upload Logo**, select one of the following:
    
    *   Upload a new logo (square or rectangular format).
        
    *   Keep the existing logo (if already uploaded).
        
5.  Review the preview of the uploaded logo.
    
6.  Select **Save** to apply the branding changes.

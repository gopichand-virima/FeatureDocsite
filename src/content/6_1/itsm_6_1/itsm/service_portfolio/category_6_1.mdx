# Category

Use this function to create categories to organize catalog items into logical groups. Categories can have a parent-child relationship, such as IT and Laptops.

In the navigation pane, select **ITSM /> Service Portfolio /> Service Catalog /> Category**.

New Category

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Category**. The New Category window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/ITSM/Service%20Portfolio/category-new-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **New**. The New Category window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/ITSM/Service%20Portfolio/category-new-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter a *Title* and *Description*.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter the *Display* order.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

In the *Parent Category* and *Location* fields, click **Addthe plus + sign**, then search for and select the Parent Category and Location.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Parent Category** is the main category to which the category belongs. For example, Laptop belongs to the Desktop category.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Location** refers to the location of the category.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

To define a specific look and feel for the category, select **Homepage Render**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

To add an image, click **Upload**, search for and select an image, then click **OK**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

Edit Category

To view a list of catalog items associated with this category, click the **Service Catalog items** tab.

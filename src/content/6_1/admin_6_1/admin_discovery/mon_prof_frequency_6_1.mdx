# Monitoring Profile: Frequency

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the *Monitoring Frequency* field, click the *Current Time Settings* drop-down list and select the applicable frequency (for example, every 30 seconds, every minute and so forth).

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/discovery-mon-prof-frequency.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" style="width: 424px;height: 269px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/discovery-mon-prof-frequency-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 427px;height: 275px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Check to select the required **Time** (Second, Minute, Hour) and **Date** (Day, Month, Weekday) for scan functionality.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Click the options to be monitored, either **System** or **File**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Click the links below to complete the other sections on this window.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="5. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="6. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Save**.

</div>

## Outage Calendar

| ![icon outage calendar](../Resources/Images/Common%20Topics/icon-outage-calendar.PNG) | Use this function to change or reschedule an outage period. When selected, the Outage Calendar displays.

- To extend the end date, drag the current end date to the new end date.
- To reschedule the start and end dates and keep the number of days intact, drag the complete outage to the new start date. |

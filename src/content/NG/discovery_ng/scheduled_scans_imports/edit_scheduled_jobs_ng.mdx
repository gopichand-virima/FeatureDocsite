# Editing Scheduled Jobs

1. Click on a scheduled job from the list.

2. The **Details** page opens with multiple tabs.

1. Make changes to any field (Name, Frequency, Timing, Credential, etc.).

2. Click **Update** or **Save** to apply changes.

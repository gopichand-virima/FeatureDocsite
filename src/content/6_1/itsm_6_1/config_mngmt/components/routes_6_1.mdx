## Add a New Route

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **Add**. The Routes dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/routes-add-new.png" original-image-title="" style="width: 473px;height: 153px;"></span>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **New Route**

</div>

<div class="NumList2" style="font-style: normal;" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter the applicable information in each field.

</div>

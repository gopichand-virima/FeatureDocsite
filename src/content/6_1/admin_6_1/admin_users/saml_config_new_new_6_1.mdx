# New SAML Configuration

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

While viewing the SAML Configuration window, click **New SAML Configuration**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Users/saml-config-add-new.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 483px;height: 76px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

In the *Name* field, enter the name of the SAML.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="3. ">

For the *Signature Algorithm*, click the drop-down list and select the required signature.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Click **Add**.

</div>

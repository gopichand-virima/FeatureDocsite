## Auto Refresh

Use this function to update the window at a specific interval of time.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Common%20Topics/auto-refresh.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI"></span>

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Common%20Topics/auto-refresh-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI"></span>

Click the *Auto Refresh* drop-down list and select from one of the following: **None, 20 seconds, 40 seconds, 60 seconds**, or **Custom** (and specify a custom refresh time).

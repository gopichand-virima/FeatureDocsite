# Purchase Order

Use this feature to configure the details of the purchase of products or services.

In the navigation pane, select **ITAM /> Procurement /> Purchase Order**. The Purchase Order window displays.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/ITAM/purchase-order-main-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

New Purchase Order

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, select **New Purchase Order**. The New Purchase Order window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Complete the fields, referring to the sections below.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**

</div>

### Purchase Order Fields

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/ITAM/Procurement_v6_1/Purchase%20Order.png" original-image-title=""></span>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Purchase Order Number**. Enter the purchase order number. This is a required field.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Purchase Order Date**. Click the **calendar icon** and select the date/time.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Vendor**. Enter the name of the vendor.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

**Assigned To**. Click **Add**, then search for and select the applicable user.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

**Assigned To**. Click the **plus + sign**, then search for and select the applicable user.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

**Ship To**. Click **Add**, then search for and select the receiver.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

**Ship To**. Click the **plus + sign**, then search for and select the receiver.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

**Bill To Location**. Click **Add**, then search for and select the location where the bill should be sent.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

**Bill to Location**. Click the **plus + sign**, then search for and select the location where the bill should be sent.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Status**. Click the drop-down list and select a *Status* for the purchase order, such as Ordered, Received, etc.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Description**. Enter information about this purchase order.

</div>

### General Fields

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/ITAM/Procurement_v6_1/General_Fields_PO.png" original-image-title=""></span>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Shipping**. Click the drop-down list and select the shipping speed.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Terms**. Click the drop-down list and select the terms for invoicing, such as 30 days, 60 days, etc.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Ship Rate**. Enter the shipping rate in a currency amount.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Total Cost**. Enter the total cost of shipping and billing.

</div>

### Details Fields

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/ITAM/Procurement_v6_1/Details_Fields_PO.jpg" original-image-title=""></span>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Initial Request**. Enter the reason for the initial request for this purchase order.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Contract**. Click **Add**, then search for and select the applicable contract.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

**Requested by**. Click **Add**, then search for and select the applicable user.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

**Requested by**. Click the **plus + sign,** then search for and select the applicable user.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

**Department**. Click **Add,** then search for and select the applicable department.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

**Department**. Click the **plus + sign**, then search for and select the applicable department.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Budget Number**. Enter the purchase order budget number.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Vendor Account**. Enter the vendor's account number.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1" style="background-color: #ffffff;">

<span style="background-color: #ffffff; color: #000000; font-weight: bold;">Received</span>.Select this checkbox only when all purchase order line items for this purchase order have been fully received. Leave it unchecked to record partial receipts by line.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Requested Date**. Enter the date/time the items on the purchase order were requested.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Expected Delivery Date**. Enter the date/time the purchase order items should be delivered.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Ordered On**. Enter the date/time the order was placed.

</div>

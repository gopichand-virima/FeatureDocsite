# Incidents

Use this function to create an incident record.

In the navigation pane, select **ITSM > Incident Management > Incidents**. The Incidents window displays.

New Incident

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Incident**.

Click **New**.

Complete the fields, referring to the information below.

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.


Once an Incident is created, a notification mail is sent to the Requester, Assignee and other Roles as per Role Access of Incident, and individual users email preferences.

# Edit Purchase Order

This feature enables editing the configured values of the existing contract, and update, and then save the same in the application.

Click to open the required Purchase Order. The Purchase Order page displays.

The Purchase Order displays following information:

- Details
- Tasks
- Comments
- Attachment
- History

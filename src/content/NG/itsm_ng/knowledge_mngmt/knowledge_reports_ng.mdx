# Knowledge Reports

Knowledge reports provide insights into content effectiveness, usage patterns, and knowledge base health.

## Standard Reports

### Article Usage Report
Track article popularity:
- Most viewed articles
- Least viewed articles
- Views over time
- User engagement

### Article Ratings Report
Quality metrics:
- Highest rated articles
- Lowest rated articles
- Average ratings by category
- Rating trends

### Knowledge Gaps Report
Identify missing content:
- High search, no results
- Unresolved incidents
- Common questions without articles
- Requested topics

### Author Productivity
Contributor metrics:
- Articles per author
- Approval rates
- Article quality scores
- Contribution trends

### Category Analysis
Content distribution:
- Articles per category
- Usage by category
- Gaps in categories
- Category growth

## Creating Custom Reports

1. Navigate to **Reports** > **Knowledge Management**
2. Click **New Report**
3. Select data fields
4. Add filters
5. Choose visualization
6. Save and schedule

## Metrics Tracked

- Total articles
- Articles published
- Articles pending
- Search queries
- Article views
- User ratings
- Resolution rates
- Time saved

# Status Update

The <span class="Strong">Status Update</span> section checks the status of devices, typically triggered during a scan.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/Discovery/IPAM-Networks/IPAM%20Functions%20Overview_1.png" original-image-title=""></span>

The available settings are:

- <span class="Strong">Probe:</span> Choose the type of scan, such as <span class="Strong">Ping Scan</span>.

- <span class="Strong">Client:</span> Select the client from the list (ensure the client is operational).

- <span class="Strong">IP Address:</span> Specify the IP address or range to scan (e.g., <span class="Strong">192.168.34.0/24</span>).

- <span class="Strong">Update Type:</span> Choose between <span class="Strong">Immediate</span> or <span class="Strong">Scheduled</span> update.

  - <span class="Strong">Immediate:</span> The scan starts right away.

  - <span class="Strong">Scheduled:</span> The scan runs at a specified time.

## Searching

Adding new or updating existing information can be done in may fields. The following is a sample.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

While viewing a Details window, locate the information to change.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Next to the field, click either the **Add** button (to add a new item) or **Update** button (to change an existing item). The applicable dialog box displays (such as the Includes Blueprint dialog box in this example).

</div>

<div class="Ind50 step-media" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20SACM/blueprints-includes-dialog-box.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 469px;height: 284px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Next to the field, click the **plus + sign**. The applicable dialog box displays (such as the Includes Blueprint dialog box in this example).

</div>

<div class="Ind50 step-media" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

[<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20SACM/blueprints-includes-dialog-box-new.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 471px;height: 314px;"></span>](../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20SACM/blueprints-includes-dialog-box-new.PNG)

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

In the *Search* field, type the name of the item to locate and click the search iconpress **Enter**. *See also* <a href="Advanced-Search.htm" style="font-style: italic;">Advanced Search</a>*.*

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Do either of the following:

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

To add an item, select it under the *Search Results* list.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

To remove an item, select it under the *Selected Items* list.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

Click Clear All to remove the selections.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="7. ">

Click **Save**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="8. ">

Click **Add**.

</div>

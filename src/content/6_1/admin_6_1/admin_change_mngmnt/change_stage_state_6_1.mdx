## Stage Functions

New Change Stage

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Change Stage**. The New Change Stage window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Change/change-stage-new-old.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **New**. The New Change Stage window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Change/change-stage-new-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter a *Name* and *Description*.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the *Stage Order* field, click the drop-down list and select the order for this stage.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Click **Add**.

</div>

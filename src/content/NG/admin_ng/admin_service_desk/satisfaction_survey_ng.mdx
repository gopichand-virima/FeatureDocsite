# Satisfaction Survey

Use this function to configure customer satisfaction surveys that gather feedback on service desk performance and ticket resolution quality.

Satisfaction surveys help measure service quality, identify areas for improvement, and track customer satisfaction metrics over time.

In the main window, click **Admin > Service Desk > Satisfaction Survey**. The Satisfaction Survey window displays.

## Survey Configuration

Configure general survey settings:

Click **Configure Survey Settings**.

**Enable Surveys**: Check to activate satisfaction surveys.

**Survey Timing**:

- **Send When**: Choose when to send surveys
  - Immediately when ticket is closed
  - X hours after ticket closure (delay to allow user to verify resolution)
  - On a scheduled basis for all closed tickets

- **Survey Window**: How long survey remains active
  - 7 days (recommended)
  - 14 days
  - 30 days
  - Custom

**Survey Frequency**:

- **Survey All Closed Tickets**: Send for every closed ticket
- **Random Sampling**: Survey X% of closed tickets
- **Priority-Based**: Survey based on ticket priority
- **VIP Only**: Survey only VIP users
- **Per User Limit**: Maximum surveys per user per month

**Reminder Settings**:

- **Send Reminder**: Remind users to complete survey
- **Reminder Timing**: Days after initial survey
- **Maximum Reminders**: Number of reminders to send

Click **Save**.

## Survey Questions

Configure the survey questions and format.

Click **Manage Survey Questions**.

### Default Rating Question

**Overall Satisfaction**:

"How satisfied were you with the support you received?"

**Rating Scale**:

- 5-point scale (Very Dissatisfied to Very Satisfied)
- Star rating (1-5 stars)
- Thumbs up/down
- Numeric scale (1-10)
- Emoji scale (ðŸ˜ž to ðŸ˜„)

### Additional Questions

Click **Add Question** to add more survey questions.

**Question Types**:

**Rating Questions**:

- "How would you rate the technician's knowledge?"
- "How would you rate the resolution time?"
- "How would you rate the communication?"

**Multiple Choice**:

- "What could we improve?"
  - Response time
  - Communication
  - Technical expertise
  - Follow-up

**Yes/No Questions**:

- "Was your issue resolved?"
- "Would you recommend our support service?"

**Open Text**:

- "Please provide any additional comments or feedback."
- "What did we do well?"
- "How can we improve?"

**Net Promoter Score (NPS)**:

- "On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend our IT support to a colleague?"

For each question, configure:

- **Question Text**: The survey question
- **Question Type**: Rating, multiple choice, text, etc.
- **Required**: Whether the question must be answered
- **Order**: Sequence in the survey

Click **Save** for each question.

## Survey Templates

Create different survey templates for different scenarios.

Click **Manage Templates**.

### New Survey Template

Click **New Template**.

Enter a *Template Name* and *Description*.

**Use Cases**:

- **Standard Incident Survey**: For general incidents
- **Request Fulfillment Survey**: For service requests
- **VIP Survey**: For high-priority users
- **Major Incident Survey**: For critical incidents
- **Project Closure Survey**: For project-related tickets

Select the questions to include in this template.

Arrange question order.

Click **Save**.

## Survey Delivery

Configure how surveys are delivered to users.

Click **Configure Delivery**.

**Delivery Methods**:

**Email Survey**:

- **Email Template**: Customize survey email
- **Subject Line**: Email subject
- **Introduction Text**: Context and purpose
- **Survey Link**: Link to web-based survey
- **Email Survey**: Embed rating directly in email

**In-App Survey**:

- Display survey when user logs into portal
- Show as notification
- Modal popup

**SMS Survey** (if configured):

- Short survey via text message
- For urgent feedback

**Survey Format**:

- **Web Form**: Full survey on dedicated page
- **Embedded**: Survey within email
- **One-Click**: Single question with quick response

Click **Save**.

## Email Template Customization

Customize the survey invitation email.

Click **Customize Email Template**.

**Subject Line**:

Default: "Please share your feedback - Ticket #{ticket_number}"

Available variables:

- `{ticket_number}`: Ticket reference
- `{ticket_subject}`: Ticket title
- `{technician_name}`: Assigned technician
- `{resolution_date}`: Date resolved

**Email Body**:

Customize the message text.

Include:

- Thank you message
- Context about the ticket
- Survey link or embedded survey
- Estimated time to complete
- Privacy statement

**Branding**:

- Company logo
- Color scheme
- Footer information

Click **Save**.

## Survey Results and Reporting

Configure how survey results are collected and reported.

Click **Configure Reporting**.

**Real-Time Notifications**:

**Low Satisfaction Alert**:

- Notify when rating is below threshold (e.g., 1 or 2 stars)
- Immediate notification to service desk manager
- Allows for follow-up with dissatisfied customers

**Reporting Dashboards**:

Configure available reports:

- **Overall Satisfaction Score**: Average rating over time
- **CSAT (Customer Satisfaction)**: Percentage satisfied/very satisfied
- **NPS (Net Promoter Score)**: Promoters minus detractors
- **Response Rate**: Percentage of users completing survey
- **Satisfaction by Technician**: Individual performance
- **Satisfaction by Category**: Performance by ticket type
- **Satisfaction Trends**: Week over week, month over month
- **Verbatim Comments**: All text feedback

**Automated Reports**:

- **Schedule**: Weekly, monthly, quarterly
- **Recipients**: Service desk manager, team leads
- **Format**: PDF, Excel, dashboard link

Click **Save**.

## Response Management

Configure how survey responses are handled.

Click **Configure Response Management**.

**Negative Feedback Follow-Up**:

- **Auto-Create Ticket**: Create follow-up ticket for low ratings
- **Assign To**: Service desk manager or quality team
- **Escalate**: Escalate based on severity of feedback
- **Alert**: Immediate notification for very low ratings

**Positive Feedback Recognition**:

- **Notify Technician**: Share positive feedback with agent
- **Manager Notification**: Alert manager of exceptional performance
- **Public Recognition**: Share in team meetings (with permission)

**Feedback Integration**:

- **Link to Ticket**: Associate survey with original ticket
- **Agent Performance**: Include in performance reviews
- **Knowledge Base**: Use feedback to improve documentation

Click **Save**.

**Trend Analysis**:

- Satisfaction over time
- Comparison by period
- Identify patterns and trends

**Detailed Analysis**:

- Filter by date range
- Filter by technician
- Filter by category
- Filter by priority
- Filter by location

**Export Data**:

- Export raw survey data
- Export for further analysis
- Integration with BI tools

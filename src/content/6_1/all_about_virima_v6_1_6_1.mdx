# About the Virima Modules and Functions

The All Modules window contains quick access to selected functions. From this main window, all functions are reached either through:

<div class="Bullet1">

The **Navigation pane** (down the left side of the window)

</div>

<div class="Bullet1" style="font-weight: normal;" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

The **Admin menu** (in the upper right corner of the window)

</div>

## Home Page/All Modules Window

The home page provides quick access to the most frequently used modules, such as favorite functions and dashboards.

<div class="Bullet1">

**Adding** a Favorite. Locate an icon in the applicable section. Then, click and drag/drop the icon into the Favorite section inside the box with the text, "Drag here to add."

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Deleting** a Favorite. Locate the icon in the Favorites section. Then, click and drag the icon above the Favorite section until the text "Drag here to delete" inside a red box displays.

</div>

## Left Navigation Pane

The left navigation pane includes the following:

<div class="Bullet1">

[My Dashboard](My%20Dashboard/My-Dashboard-Both.htm) displays data content information with a customization option.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[CMDB](CMDB%20-%20Left%20Nav/CMDB-Overview.htm) provides a centralized repository for Configuration Items (CIs) and their relationships, with detailed CI profiles (attributes, ownership, linked components, dependencies, audit history, and lifecycle).

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Discovery Scan](Discovery/About-Discovery-Scan.htm) manages scanning activities (running, scheduling, etc.) and importing data (such as from AWS and Azure).

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[ITAM](ITAM/About-ITAM.htm) (asset management functions) and [ITSM](ITSM/About-ITSM.htm) (service management functions)

</div>

|     |                                                                                    |
|-----|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|     | The menu heading displayed - either ITAM or ITSM - is based upon the subscription. |

<div class="Bullet1">

[Vulnerability Management](Vulnerability%20Managment/Vulnerability-Management.htm) identifies, evaluates, and reports security vulnerabilities in CIs present inside the CMDB.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Self Service](Self%20Service/About-Self-Service.htm) creates catalog items and shows incidents and requests for the current user.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Project and Program Management](Prog-Proj%20Mngmnt/About-Prog-Proj-Mngmnt.htm) defines project aspects and managing related projects.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Risk Register](Risk%20Register/About-Risk-Register.htm) provides a tool for managing risk and compliance.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Reports](Reports/Reports.htm) generates both canned and custom ad hoc reports.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**<a href="MSP/About-MSP.htm" style="font-weight: normal;">MSP</a>** displays a list of clients and includes a Dashboard, Reports, My Incidents, and Administrative functions (Client, Users, Mailbox Monitoring Configuration, and Email Templates). It also provides access to the Virima application.

</div>

## Admin Window

The administrative functions include the following:

<div class="Bullet1">

[Organizational Details](Admin%20-%20Org%20Details/About-Org-Details.htm) specifies business-related details, such as departments, cost centers, locations, designations, operational hours, and holidays.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Discovery](Admin%20-%20Discovery/Admin-Discovery.htm) configures clients, defines credentials and major software, manages processes, and other discovery-related functionality (scan configuration, monitoring profile, and application mapping).

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[SACM](Admin%20-%20SACM/Admin-SACM.htm) handles blueprints, relationship types, confidence configuration, and software-related functions.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Users](Admin%20-%20Users/Admin-Users.htm) creates users, roles, groups, and time tracking reports. Also allows for AD import and authentication, Azure AD and SAML configuration.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Management Functions](Admin/Admin-Mngmnt-Functions.htm) for [Changes](Admin%20-%20Change%20Mngmnt/About-Change-Mngmnt.htm), [Contracts](Admin%20-%20Contract%20Mngmt/About-Contract-Mngmnt.htm), [Events](Admin%20-%20Event%20Mngmnt/About-Event-Mngmnt.htm), [Hardware Assets](Admin%20-%20Hardware%20Asset%20Mngmnt/About-HW-Asset-Mngmnt.htm), [Incidents](Admin%20-%20Incident%20Mngmnt/About-Incident-Mngmnt.htm), [Knowledge](Admin%20-%20Knowledge%20Mngmnt/About-Knowledge-Mngmnt.htm), [Problems](Admin%20-%20Problem%20Mngmnt/About-Problem-Mngmnt.htm), [Projects](Admin%20-%20Project%20Mngmnt/About-Project-Management.htm), [Releases](Admin%20-%20Release%20Mngmnt/About-Release-Mngmnt.htm), and [Requests](Admin%20-%20Request%20Mngmnt/About-Request-Mngmnt.htm).

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Integrations](Admin%20-%20Integrations/About-Integrations.htm) provides credential and mapping information for Cherwell, Ivanti, ServiceNow, and Jira Credentials and Infoblox configuration.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

[Others](Admin%20-%20Other/Admin-Other.htm) includes functions such as Announcements, KPIs, Business Rules, Role Access, and SMTP configuration.

</div>

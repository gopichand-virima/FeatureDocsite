# VLANs

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

## Add a New VLAN

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **Add**. The VLAN dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/vlan-add-new.png" original-image-title="" style="width: 473px;height: 211px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **New VLAN.**

</div>

<div class="NumList2" style="font-style: normal;" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter the applicable information in the fields.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

When all selections are made, click **Save**.

</div>

## Modify Existing VLAN

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **Existing VLAN**

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/vlan-existing-new.png" original-image-title="" style="width: 474px;height: 324px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Enter the search criteria to locate the existing device.  *See also* <a href="../../../Common%20Topics/Advanced-Search.htm" style="font-style: italic;">Advanced Search</a>*.*

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Select the applicable device from the results list.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

When all sections are made, click **Save**.

</div>

## Delete VLAN

While viewing the list of existing devices, select the device to delete, and click the **Delete** button.

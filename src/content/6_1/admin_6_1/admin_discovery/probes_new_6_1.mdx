# <span style="background-color: #ffffff;">New Probes</span>

<div class="numlist1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, select **Admin /> Discovery /> Probes**. The Probes window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/probes-list.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" style="width: 621px;height: 146px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

<span class="span_1">From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Probes**. The New Probes window displays.</span>

</div>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, click the **gear** icon and select **Discovery /> Probes**. The Probes window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/probes-list-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 624px;height: 125px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **New Probe**. The New Probe window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Discovery/probe-new-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 623px;height: 130px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Enter a *Name* for the probe.

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

From the drop-down lists, select the *Type* and *Sensor*.

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Enter a *Description*.

</div>

<div class="numlist2" madcap:autonum="6. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

# Ad Hoc Reports

Use this function to generate ad hoc reports at a scheduled time, eliminating the manual effort otherwise required.

Once generated, these reports can then be marked as [Canned Reports](/reports/reports_canned_5_13.mdx) and displayed on the dashboard or exported to a spreadsheet.

In the navigation pane, select **Reports > Ad Hoc Reports**. The Reports window displays.

New Ad Hoc Report

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose [New Report](/reports/reports_new_ad_hoc_5_13.mdx).

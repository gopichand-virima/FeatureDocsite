# Moving Assets to CMBD

[Use this function to view the assets discovered after the scan and move them to the CMDB.]{.span_5}

+:-:+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Column 1 | An asset record can also be moved from the Details window. However, wait until the scan is complete before initiating a move operation. Also, once a record is moved, it cannot be retrieved. |
| --- | --- |
| | If the move appears to be unsuccessful, contact your System Administrator. |
+---+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

 -- ----- --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1. In the navigation pane, select [Discovery Scan]{.Strong_4}[ \> ]{.span_5}[Discovered Items]{.Strong_4}. The Discovered Items window displays.
 -- ----- --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: Ind50
![discovered items main new 621x107](../Resources/Images/Discovery/discovered-items-main-new_621x107.png)[Yes]{.Strong_4} (to move the asset) or **No** (to cancel the action). The discovered assets are moved to the CMDB.
 -- ----- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 -- ----- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 5. [In the Confirmation window, click ]{.span_5}[Continue]{.Strong_4} (to move the asset) or **Cancel** (to cancel the action). The discovered assets are moved to the CMDB.
 -- ----- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

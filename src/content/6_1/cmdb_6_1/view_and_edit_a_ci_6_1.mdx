# Viewing and Editing a CI

Each <span class="Strong" style="font-weight: bold;">Configuration Item (CI)</span> in the Virima CMDB features a <span class="Strong">dedicated record view</span> that displays all critical data related to that CI, organized into tabs. This setup enables users to analyze, update, and manage the CI from a single screen.

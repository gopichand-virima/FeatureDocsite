# Deleting Scheduled Jobs

1. Select one or more scheduled jobs.

2. From the **Select Actions** dropdown, choose **Delete**.

1. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

**Effect:**

- The scheduled job is permanently removed.
- Future executions will not occur.
- Past scan/import results in **Recent Scans** or **Import** pages remain unaffected.

## Re-Scan

To rescan a list item, do either of the following:

<div class="Bullet1">

Click the **Refresh** icon at the end of the field (if available).

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, **select Re-scan**.

</div>

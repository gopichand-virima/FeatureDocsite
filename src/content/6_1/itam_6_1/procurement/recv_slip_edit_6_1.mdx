# Edit Receiving Slip

This feature enables editing the configured values of the existing contract, and update, and then save the same in the application.

Click to open the required Receiving Slip. The Receiving Slip page displays.

The Receiving Slip displays the following information:

- Details
- Tasks
- Comments
- Attachment
- History

## Send Report To

Use to specify the users, group, roles or departments to whom the report is sent.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click the applicable tab.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Search for the user, group, role or department. The results are displayed. To enter additional search criteria, click [Advanced Search](Advanced-Search.htm).

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

From the list, select all the relevant name. As names are selected, they are moved to the Selected column.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

When all selections are made, click **Save**.

</div>

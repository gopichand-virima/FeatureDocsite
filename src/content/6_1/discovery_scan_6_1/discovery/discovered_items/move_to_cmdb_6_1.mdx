# <span class="Strong">Move to CMDB</span>

- Select one or more checkboxes

- Click on <span class="Strong">Select Actions</span> and then Select <span class="Strong">Move to CMDB</span>.

- A confirmation pop up as below is displayed

  - Click <span class="Strong">Yes</span> to move the asset to CMDB

    - Asset will be “greyed out” in Discovered Items list indicating the move is in progress and a spinner is shown to indicate move is in process

    - Once moved, asset is removed from Discovered items and will appear in CMDB

  - Click <span class="Strong">No </span>to retain the asset in Discovered Items and move it later

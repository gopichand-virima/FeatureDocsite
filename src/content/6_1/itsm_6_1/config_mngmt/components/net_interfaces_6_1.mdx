<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **Add**. The Network Interface dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/Components/network-interfaces-add-new.png" original-image-title="" style="width: 473px;height: 274px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" style="font-style: normal;" madcap:autonum="3. ">

# My Incidents

Use this function to view and manage incidents.

- Log into MSP.
- Click **My Incidents**. A list of all incidents is displayed.

!

- To view the details for an incident, click the applicable line item. *Refer to* [Incidents](/itsm/incident_mngmt/incidents_6_1.mdx)*.*

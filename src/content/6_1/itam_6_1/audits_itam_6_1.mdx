From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Audit**.

</div>

<div class="NumList1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

Click **New**.

</div>

</div>

<div class="Ind50 step-media" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

[<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/ITAM/audits-new-new.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>](../Resources/Images/ITAM/audits-new-new.PNG)

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Complete the fields, referring to the information below.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Name**. Enter the name for this audit.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Description**. Enter the details that describe this audit.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

**Auditor**. Click **Add**, then search for and select the applicable user(s).

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

**Auditor**. Click the **plus + sign**, then search for and select the applicable user(s).

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

**Checked By**. Click **Add**, then search for and select the applicable user(s).

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

**Checked By**. Click the **plus + sign**, then search for and select the applicable user(s).

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Recurring Frequency**. Click the drop-down list and select a time frame, such as Daily, Weekly, etc.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Duration** (in days). Enter the number of days for the audit to take place.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2">

**Audit Results**. Click the drop-down list and select the outcome of the audit. such as *Pass*, *Fail*. or *In Progress*.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

**Used by Hardware Assets**. Click **Add**, then search for and select the applicable hardware assets for this audit.

</div>

<div class="Bullet2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

**Used by Hardware Assets**. Click the **plus + sign**, then search for and select the applicable hardware assets for this audit.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

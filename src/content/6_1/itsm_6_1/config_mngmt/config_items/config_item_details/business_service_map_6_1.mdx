# Business Service Map

Use the Business Service Map to graphically display the configuration items that support a business service. For example, delivering email to employees of an organization is supported by exchange servers and web servers. Below is an example.

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/Biz-Svc-Map/bsm-example.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

**Type**. Click the drop-down list and select the type of view.

<div class="Bullet1">

**<a href="BSM%20Examples/BSM-ADM-View.htm" style="font-weight: normal;">ADM View</a>** displays the graphical connection between the applications or between applications and configuration items based on the relationship *Communicates With*. Administrators can add relations manually. ADM is processed automatically when the discovered items are moved into the CMDB. SSH communication is ignored in the ADM.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1" style="color: #000000;">

**Affected Business Application View** displays the list of services/applications impacted due to an affected Business Service.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**<a href="BSM%20Examples/BSM-Comm-View.htm" target="_popup" style="font-weight: normal;">Communication View</a>** displays the graphical connection between the configuration items based on the relationship *Communicates With*. To view the list of Windows Host, click on the number. Grayed out machines/devices can be of different network or the devices that are not discovered by the Discovery application.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1" style="color: #000000;">

<span style="color: #dc143c;"> <span style="color: #000000;"> **Infrastructure View** displays the end-to-end visualization of a Server/Application infrastructure.</span> </span>

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**<a href="BSM%20Examples/BSM-Network-View.htm" style="font-weight: normal;">Network View</a>** displays the physical and non-physical connections among the assets and/or business services based on relationships.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Service Topology View** displays the end-to-end visualization of a running service.

</div>

**Select Actions**. Click the drop-down list and select the action.

<div class="Bullet1">

**Rescan.** When selected. displays the Rescan Now dialog box. Choose a *Client* and the *Type of Scan*. Then click **Scan**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/rescan-now-dialog-box.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 476px;height: 212px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Host Check**. When selected, displays the Check Host Reachability dialog box. Enter the *IPÂ Address* and *Choose a client*, then click **Scan**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/Biz-Svc-Map/check-host-reachability.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 473px;height: 216px;"></span>

</div>

**Orientation**. Select *Horizontal*, **View** or *Radical* to change the perspective of the map.

**Refresh**. Updates the display to reflect any map changes.

**Additional Settings**. Click the red circle with the pencil in the lower right corner of the map.

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/Biz-Svc-Map/bsm-edit-options.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Level Depth**. Specifies the number of levels deep to go to see the details. The maximum depth level is 5.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Show Legends On Bottom**. Specifies where the legend is located and if the filtered items should be removed.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Filter**. Filters the data in the map. In the Filter dialog box, select which items to display.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Full Screen**. Toggles between full screen and normal view.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Download BSM**. Displays the Download BSM View dialog box in which you specify what type of file to download. There must be data in order for the download to occur. Click either **Save as PNG** (image) or **Save Data as Excel** (spreadsheet), then click **Download**.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Save View**. Saves the current view, which can be reloaded for reference at another time.

</div>

<div class="Bullet1">

**Load View**. Loads any saved views.

</div>

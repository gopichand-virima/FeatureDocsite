# Move items to the CMDB

After importing AWS data, you may promote it to the CMDB.

1.  <span class="inline-comment-marker">In </span><span class="Strong">All</span><span class="inline-comment-marker">, </span><span class="Strong">Authorized</span><span class="inline-comment-marker"> or </span><span class="Strong">Unauthorized</span><span class="inline-comment-marker">, select one or more rows.</span>

2.  Click <span class="Strong">Move to CMDB</span>.

    <span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../Resources/Images/Discovery/Import-From-AWS/Import%20From%20AWS_1_8.png" original-image-title=""></span>

<!-- -->

3.  In <span class="inline-comment-marker">the confirmation dialog, click </span><span class="Strong">Continue</span><span class="inline-comment-marker">.</span>  
    <span class="inline-comment-marker">The move job runs </span><span class="Strong">in the background</span><span class="inline-comment-marker">—no further action is required. Use </span><span class="Strong">Auto Refresh</span><span class="inline-comment-marker"> to watch status changes.</span>

Tip: If no rows are selected, your environment will move <span class="Strong">all records that match the current filters</span>. If you want to limit scope, always select the exact rows to move.

4.  Upon successful move to CMDB, the records will be deleted from “Import from AWS” and “Discovered Items” View.

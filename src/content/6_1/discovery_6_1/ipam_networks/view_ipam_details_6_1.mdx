# View IPAM Details

- To view details for the selected IP, click the **Details** tab.
- To view schedule details, click the **Schedule Details** tab.
- To view the history of activity, such as when changes were made, click the **History** tab.

The Details tab provides comprehensive information about the selected subnet or IP address, including:
- Network address and subnet mask
- CIDR notation
- Gateway address
- DHCP range (if configured)
- Total addresses in the subnet
- Used vs. available addresses
- Utilization percentage
- Associated VLAN information
- Location and site details
- Comments and custom attributes

# Known Errors

Use this function to create and manage known errors.

In the navigation pane, select **ITSM > Problem Management > Known Errors**. The Known Errors window displays.

New Known Error

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Known Error**.

Click **New**.

Complete the fields.

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

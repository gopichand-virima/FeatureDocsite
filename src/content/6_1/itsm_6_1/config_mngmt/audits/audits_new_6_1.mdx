# New Audit

Use this function to add an audit process.

<div class="numlist1" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the navigation pane, select **ITSM /> Configuration Management /> Audits**. The Audits window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New**. The New Audits window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/audits-old-add.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 573px;height: 177px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="numlist1" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the navigation pane, select **Configuration Management /> Audits**. The Audits window displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="2. ">

Click **New Audit** to add a new audits process. The New Audit window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/audits-new-add.png" original-image-title="" style="width: 579px;height: 236px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

In the *Name* field, type a name for this audit process.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

In the *Description* field, type a description.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="5. ">

In the *Auditor* field, click **Add**. The Auditor dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/auditor-search.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 331px;height: 117px;" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="6. ">

In the *Auditor* field, click **+** to add an auditor. The Auditor dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/auditor-search-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 326px;height: 300px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumLista" madcap:autonum="a. ">

In the *Search* field, type all or part of the name to search for. *See also* <a href="../../../Common%20Topics/Advanced-Search.htm" target="_popup" style="font-style: italic;">Advanced Search</a>

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="b. ">

When the applicable auditor displays, click the radio button next to the auditor's name.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="c. ">

Click **Save**.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="d. ">

Click **Add**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="7. ">

In the *Checked By* field, click **Add+**. The Checked By dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/checked-by-search.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 471px;height: 215px;" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/checked-by-search-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 471px;height: 434px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumLista" madcap:autonum="a. ">

In the *Search* field, type all or part of the name to search for. *See also* <a href="../../../Common%20Topics/Advanced-Search.htm" target="_popup" style="font-style: italic;">Advanced Search</a>*.*

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="b. ">

Click the **Search** icon.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="c. ">

When the applicable auditor displays, click the radio button next to the auditor's name.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI" madcap:autonum="d. ">

Click **Save**.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="e. ">

Click **Add**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" style="color: #000000;" madcap:autonum="8. ">

Select **Run a Scan** to trigger a validation type probe scan on a device. Based on the result of the scan, the audit is marked as passed or failed.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="9. ">

From the *Audit Result* drop-down list, choose **Pass**, **Fail** or **In Progress**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="10. ">

From the *Select Probe* drop-down list, choose the applicable option.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" madcap:autonum="11. ">

From the *Scan Frequency* drop-down list, choose the applicable frequency of the scan to be performed.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="12. ">

To add configuration items for this audit, in the Configuration Items section, click **Add**. The Configuration Items dialog box displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/config-items-audit.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 471px;height: 313px;" madcap:conditions="Default.OldUI"></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../../../Resources/Images/ITSM/config-items-audit-new.PNG" original-image-title="" madcap:conditions="Default.NewUI" style="width: 470px;height: 380px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="13. ">

In the *Blueprint* field, click the drop-down list and select the applicable blueprint.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="14. ">

In the *Search* field, enter the search criteria. *See also* <a href="../../../Common%20Topics/Advanced-Search.htm" target="_popup" style="font-style: italic;">Advanced Search</a>*.*

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="15. ">

Under the *Search Results*, select the applicable search result(s) to move it to the *Selected Items* list.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="16. ">

Click **Save**. The added configuration item displays.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="17. ">

Add any additional configuration items.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="18. ">

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

</div>

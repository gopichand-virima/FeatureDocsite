# Problems

Use this function to create and manage problem records.

In the navigation pane, select **ITSM > Problem Management > Problems**. The Problems window displays.

New Problem

From the *Select Actions* drop-down list, choose **New Problem**.

Click **New**.

Complete the fields.

When all selections/entries are made, click **Add**.

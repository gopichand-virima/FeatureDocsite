## Admin Window

The administrative functions include the following:

::: Bullet1
[Organizational Details](Admin%20-%20Org%20Details/About-Org-Details.mdx) specifies business-related details, such as departments, cost centers, locations, designations, operational hours, and holidays.

::: Bullet1
[Discovery](Admin%20-%20Discovery/Admin-Discovery.mdx) configures clients, defines credentials and major software, manages processes, and other discovery-related functionality (scan configuration, monitoring profile, and application mapping).

::: Bullet1
[SACM](Admin%20-%20SACM/Admin-SACM.mdx) handles blueprints, relationship types, confidence configuration, and software-related functions.

::: Bullet1
[Users](Admin%20-%20Users/Admin-Users.mdx) creates users, roles, groups, and time tracking reports. Also allows for AD import and authentication, Azure AD and SAML configuration.

::: Bullet1
[Management Functions](Admin/Admin-Mngmnt-Functions.mdx) for [Changes](Admin%20-%20Change%20Mngmnt/About-Change-Mngmnt.mdx), [Contracts](Admin%20-%20Contract%20Mngmt/About-Contract-Mngmnt.mdx), [Events](Admin%20-%20Event%20Mngmnt/About-Event-Mngmnt.mdx), [Hardware Assets](Admin%20-%20Hardware%20Asset%20Mngmnt/About-HW-Asset-Mngmnt.mdx), [Incidents](Admin%20-%20Incident%20Mngmnt/About-Incident-Mngmnt.mdx), [Knowledge](Admin%20-%20Knowledge%20Mngmnt/About-Knowledge-Mngmnt.mdx), [Problems](Admin%20-%20Problem%20Mngmnt/About-Problem-Mngmnt.mdx), [Projects](Admin%20-%20Project%20Mngmnt/About-Project-Management.mdx), [Releases](Admin%20-%20Release%20Mngmnt/About-Release-Mngmnt.mdx), and [Requests](Admin%20-%20Request%20Mngmnt/About-Request-Mngmnt.mdx).

::: Bullet1
[Integrations](Admin%20-%20Integrations/About-Integrations.mdx) provides credential and mapping information for Cherwell, Ivanti, ServiceNow, and Jira Credentials and Infoblox configuration.

::: Bullet1
[Others](Admin%20-%20Other/Admin-Other.mdx) includes functions such as Announcements, KPIs, Business Rules, Role Access, and SMTP configuration.

# Risk Score Calculator

Use this function to assign a score to a Risk based on Probability and Impact as defined by the Admin.

In the main window, click **Admin > Others > Risk Score Calculator**. The Risk Score Calculation window displays.

New Risk Score Calculator

Enter the applicable values by clicking the drop-down list arrow and selecting an available choice.

When all selections/entries are made, click **Save**.

Edit Risk Score Calculator

In the main window, make the necessary changes.

When all selections/entries are made, click **Save**.

Delete Risk Score Calculator

A Risk Score Calculator cannot be deleted, only edited.

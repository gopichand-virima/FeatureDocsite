# New Ivanti Mappings

Use this function to enter the details for mapping properties between Virima and Ivanti.

<div class="NumList1" madcap:autonum="1. ">

In the main window, click the **gear** icon and select **Integrations /> Ivanti Mappings**.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/icon-invanti.PNG" original-image-title=""></span>

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

The Ivanti Mappings window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/ivanti-mappings.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 621px;height: 118px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="2. ">

To create a new mapping, click **New Mapping**. The New Ivanti Mapping window displays.

</div>

<div class="Ind50">

<span class="image placeholder" original-image-src="../Resources/Images/Admin%20-%20Integration/new-ivanti-mapping-new.PNG" original-image-title="" style="width: 619px;height: 229px;"></span>

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="3. ">

Click the drop-down lists and select the **Ivanti CI Type**, **CI SubType** and **CI Category**.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="4. ">

Under Virima Blueprint, do the following:

</div>

<div class="NumLista" madcap:autonum="a. ">

Select the **Property**, **Operator**, and **Value**.

</div>

<div class="NumListb" madcap:autonum="b. ">

Select the type of condition (and, or).

</div>

<div class="NumListb" madcap:autonum="c. ">

Select the **Property**, **Operator**, and **Value**.

</div>

<div class="NumListb" madcap:autonum="d. ">

To delete a condition line, click the trashcan icon.

</div>

<div class="NumList2" madcap:autonum="5. ">

Under Ivanti Properties, do the following:

</div>

<div class="NumLista" madcap:autonum="a. ">

Click the Add button. A new line displays.

</div>

<div class="NumListb" madcap:autonum="b. ">

In the *Virima Property* field, click the drop-down list and select an existing property.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="c. ">

In the *Ivanti Property* field, click the drop-down list and select an existing property.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="d. ">

In the *Value* field, enter the value to associate with the property.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="e. ">

To use a correlator, check Correlator.

</div>

<div class="numListb" madcap:autonum="f. ">

To delete an item, select the property line and click the trashcan icon.

</div>

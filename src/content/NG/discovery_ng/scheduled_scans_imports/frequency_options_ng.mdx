# Frequency Options

Scheduled jobs can be configured with the following frequency options:

- **Every Day** – Runs daily at the specified time
- **Every 2 Days** – Runs every other day
- **Every Week** – Runs once per week on a specific day
- **Every 2 Weeks** – Runs biweekly
- **Every Month** – Runs monthly on a specific date
- **Custom Intervals** – Define custom recurring schedules (e.g., every 3 days, every 12 hours)
